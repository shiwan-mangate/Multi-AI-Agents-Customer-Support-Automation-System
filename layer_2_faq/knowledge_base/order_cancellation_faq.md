## SECTION 1 — GENERAL CANCELLATION ELIGIBILITY

---

**Q: Can I cancel my order after placing it?**
A: Yes, orders can be cancelled, but only within a limited window before they enter the fulfillment process. Once an order has been picked, packed, or handed to a carrier, it can no longer be cancelled directly. The ability to cancel depends on how quickly you act after placing the order. If cancellation is no longer possible, you may need to wait for delivery and then initiate a return under our Return Policy.

---

**Q: How long do I have to cancel an order after placing it?**
A: You typically have up to 1 hour from the time of order placement to cancel before the order enters active processing. During peak periods (sales events, holidays, high-volume days), this window may be shorter — sometimes as little as 15–30 minutes — due to accelerated warehouse processing. We strongly recommend submitting a cancellation request as quickly as possible after deciding you no longer want the order.

---

**Q: What orders are eligible for cancellation?**
A: An order is eligible for cancellation if:
- It was placed within the last hour and has not yet entered the packing stage
- It contains items that are not custom-made, personalized, or made-to-order
- It has not been flagged as a fraud-prevention hold (which temporarily locks the order)
- It is not a same-day or express delivery order that has already been dispatched
- It is not a digital product order where the license or download link has already been delivered

---

**Q: What orders cannot be cancelled?**
A: The following orders cannot be cancelled once placed:
- Orders that have already been packed or shipped
- Custom, personalized, or made-to-order items (production begins immediately upon order confirmation)
- Digital products and software licenses where the activation key or download link has already been sent
- Same-day delivery orders that are already in transit with a courier
- Pre-orders that have entered the shipment processing stage
- Orders placed with an instant-delivery merchant (marketplace items shipped and fulfilled by a third-party seller)
- Orders subject to an active fraud hold (these require resolution before any action can be taken)

---

**Q: Can I cancel only part of an order?**
A: Yes, partial cancellations are supported if the order has not yet been fully packed. You can request to cancel one or more specific items from a multi-item order while keeping the rest. If the items you wish to cancel have already been packed together with the rest of the order, a full cancellation may be the only option. Contact our support team immediately and specify which items you wish to cancel.

---

**Q: Can I cancel a order placed through the mobile app?**
A: Yes. Orders placed through our mobile app can be cancelled the same way as website orders. Open the app, go to "My Orders," select the relevant order, and tap "Cancel Order" if the option is available. If the cancellation option is greyed out or not visible, the order has already progressed to a stage where app-based self-cancellation is not possible — contact support immediately via the in-app chat.

---

**Q: Can I cancel a marketplace or third-party seller order?**
A: Orders fulfilled by third-party sellers on our marketplace are subject to the seller's individual cancellation policy, not ours. Cancellation requests for marketplace orders should be directed to the seller through the messaging system in your order details. If the seller does not respond within 24 hours or refuses a legitimate cancellation, contact our marketplace support team for mediation.

## SECTION 2 — HOW TO CANCEL AN ORDER

---

**Q: How do I cancel an order?**
A: To cancel an order, use any of the following methods — the fastest available being the account portal:

1. **Account Portal (fastest):** Log in → go to "My Orders" → select the order → click "Cancel Order" → confirm the cancellation. This is available only if the order is still in a cancellable state.
2. **Live Chat:** Open the live chat widget on our website and type "cancel order [order number]." A support agent or automated system can process it instantly if the order is still eligible.
3. **Email:** Send a cancellation request to cancel@company.com with your order number in the subject line. Processed within 15–30 minutes during business hours, but not recommended for urgent cancellations given processing delays.
4. **Phone:** Call 1-800-XXX-XXXX and select the order cancellation option. Fastest human-assisted method if the online portal is unavailable.

---

**Q: What happens after I submit a cancellation request?**
A: After submitting a cancellation request:
1. Our system checks whether the order is still in a cancellable state.
2. If eligible, the cancellation is confirmed immediately and you receive a cancellation confirmation email.
3. The payment hold or charge is reversed (see refund section for timelines).
4. If the order is already in packing or shipping, you will receive a notification that the cancellation could not be processed and instructions for returning the item after delivery.

---

**Q: I submitted a cancellation request but didn't receive a confirmation. Was it processed?**
A: A cancellation confirmation email is sent within 5 minutes of a successful cancellation. If you have not received one within 10 minutes, check your spam or junk folder. If it is not there, log in to your account and check the order status — if it still shows "Processing" or "Pending," the cancellation may not have gone through. Contact support immediately via live chat to confirm.

---

**Q: Can I cancel an order without logging into my account?**
A: Yes, if you placed the order as a guest. Use our Order Management Portal at orders.company.com, enter your order number and the email address used at checkout, and follow the cancellation steps. Alternatively, contact our support team via live chat or phone with your order number and we will process the cancellation on your behalf after verifying your identity.

---

**Q: What is the fastest way to cancel an order?**
A: The fastest way to cancel is through the self-service "Cancel Order" button in your account portal or through live chat. Both methods can process a cancellation in real time if the order is still eligible. Email and phone (during busy periods) may take longer. Do not rely on email cancellation requests for time-sensitive situations — use the portal or live chat.

---

**Q: Can a support agent cancel my order even if the portal says it cannot be cancelled?**
A: Support agents have the ability to escalate a cancellation attempt to our warehouse team, who may be able to intercept the order before it leaves the facility. This is not guaranteed and depends on how far along in the packing process the order is. If you contact us within 30 minutes of placing the order and it hasn't been packed yet, an agent may be able to manually stop it. There are no guarantees once packing has begun.

## SECTION 3 — CANCELLATION AFTER SHIPMENT

---

**Q: What happens if I request a cancellation but the order has already shipped?**
A: If your order has already been shipped, the cancellation cannot be processed. You will receive a notification explaining this, along with the following options:
- **Refuse delivery:** If you are present when the carrier attempts delivery, you can refuse to accept the package. It will be returned to our warehouse and a refund will be issued once received and inspected.
- **Return after delivery:** Accept the delivery and initiate a return within the 30-day return window under our Return Policy.
- **Carrier intercept:** In some cases, we can request a package intercept or redirect through the carrier (FedEx or UPS). This is not guaranteed and may carry an additional fee of $10–$20, which will be deducted from your refund.

---

**Q: How do I refuse delivery to cancel a shipped order?**
A: To refuse delivery:
- If a signature is required: simply tell the delivery driver you refuse the package. They will mark it as refused and return it to the sender.
- If no signature is required and the package was left at your door: do not open it. Contact the carrier using the tracking number to schedule a pickup for return to sender, or take the unopened package to a carrier drop-off location.
- Once the returned package is received and inspected at our warehouse, a full refund will be processed within 5–7 business days.

---

**Q: Can I cancel an order that shows "Out for Delivery"?**
A: Once an order is "Out for Delivery," it is in the carrier's active delivery run and cannot be recalled or intercepted. Your options at this point are to refuse the package at the door (if you are present) or to accept delivery and initiate a return. If you are not home and the package is left at your door, contact the carrier as soon as possible about a return pickup, but do not open the package to preserve return eligibility.

---

**Q: Will I be charged return shipping if my cancellation is rejected because the order has already shipped?**
A: No. If you submitted a cancellation request in good faith and we were unable to stop the shipment in time, we will provide a prepaid return shipping label so you can send the item back at no cost. This applies only when the cancellation request was submitted before we notified you the order had shipped. If you request a return after being notified of shipment, standard return shipping rules apply.

---

**Q: What if I receive the order and it is no longer needed? Can I still cancel it?**
A: Once delivered, "cancellation" is no longer applicable — the appropriate process is a return. You can initiate a return within 30 days of delivery under our Return Policy. Eligible items returned in original condition will receive a full refund. See our Return Policy FAQ for detailed guidance on how to initiate a return.

## SECTION 4 — CANCELLATION REFUNDS

---

**Q: When will I receive my refund after a cancellation?**
A: Refund timelines after a successful cancellation depend on how far along the order was and your payment method:

| Scenario | Refund Timeline |
|---|---|
| Cancelled before payment captured (authorization only) | Authorization released within 1–3 business days |
| Cancelled after payment captured, before shipment | 3–5 business days to original payment method |
| Cancelled after shipment (via return/refusal) | 5–10 business days after item is received and inspected |
| Cancelled digital product (before download/access) | 1–2 business days |

---

**Q: Will I receive a full refund when I cancel an order?**
A: Yes, a full refund of the purchase price including any shipping fees paid is issued for cancellations processed before the order ships. If the order was cancelled after shipment via package refusal or return, shipping charges may be non-refundable unless the cancellation request was submitted before we confirmed shipment.

---

**Q: The payment was only an authorization hold, not a full charge. How does the refund work?**
A: When you place an order, we place an authorization hold on your payment method to reserve the funds. If your order is cancelled before the hold is captured (converted to a full charge), we release the authorization. This typically appears as the hold disappearing from your statement within 1–5 business days, depending on your bank. No actual charge was made, so no formal refund transaction occurs — the hold simply expires.

---

**Q: My bank still shows the charge even though my cancellation was confirmed. What should I do?**
A: This is a common situation. When an order is cancelled, we release the payment authorization or initiate a refund immediately, but your bank may take 1–7 business days to reflect this on your statement. Check your account again after 5 business days. If the charge is still showing after 7 business days with a confirmed cancellation email, contact your bank directly with the cancellation confirmation and they can expedite the release. You can also contact our support team for a refund reference number.

---

**Q: Will my refund be returned to the original payment method?**
A: Yes, refunds are always returned to the original payment method used at checkout. If that method is no longer valid (expired card, closed account), contact our support team and we will arrange an alternative refund method such as bank transfer or store credit. For orders paid with store credit or gift cards, the refund is returned as store credit to your account.

---

**Q: Can I receive my cancellation refund as store credit instead?**
A: Yes. If you prefer store credit instead of a refund to your original payment method, let our support team know at the time of cancellation. Store credit is applied to your account immediately upon cancellation confirmation, compared to the 3–5 business days for card refunds. Some promotions offer a bonus of 5–10% if you opt for store credit instead of a cash refund.

---

**Q: I used a promo code on my cancelled order. Will the code be reinstated?**
A: In most cases, promo codes used on cancelled orders are not automatically reinstated. However, if the cancellation was due to our error (wrong item, stock issue, etc.) or a system error, contact our support team and we will manually reissue the promo code. For customer-initiated cancellations (change of mind), code reinstatement is at our discretion and evaluated case by case.

---

**Q: I paid using a buy-now-pay-later (BNPL) service. How does the cancellation refund work?**
A: For BNPL orders (Klarna, Afterpay, Affirm, etc.) that are cancelled, we notify the BNPL provider of the cancellation. The provider will then void the outstanding payment schedule or reverse any payments already made. The adjustment typically takes 3–10 business days to appear in your BNPL account. If any installment has already been debited from your bank, the BNPL provider will refund that installment according to their own refund timeline. Contact the BNPL provider directly with your cancellation confirmation if the adjustment doesn't appear.

## SECTION 5 — PRE-ORDER CANCELLATIONS

---

**Q: Can I cancel a pre-order?**
A: Yes. Pre-orders can be cancelled at any time for a full refund, right up until the point the pre-order enters the shipment processing stage. Once we begin preparing your pre-ordered item for dispatch, cancellations are subject to the same rules as regular in-process orders. To cancel a pre-order, log in to "My Orders," select the pre-order, and click "Cancel Pre-Order," or contact support.

---

**Q: Will I get a full refund if I cancel a pre-order?**
A: Yes. Pre-order cancellations at any stage before shipment processing entitle you to a full refund of any amount already paid, including any pre-order deposit. Refunds are returned to the original payment method within 3–5 business days.

---

**Q: What if the pre-ordered item is significantly delayed? Can I cancel without penalty?**
A: Yes. If a pre-ordered item's estimated release or ship date is delayed by more than 30 days beyond the original estimate communicated at the time of pre-order, you are entitled to cancel the pre-order for a full refund, regardless of whether the order has entered fulfillment. We will notify you by email if a significant delay occurs, and the cancellation option will remain available for a minimum of 14 days after the delay notification.

---

**Q: What if a pre-ordered item is discontinued or no longer available?**
A: If a pre-ordered item is discontinued, out of stock indefinitely, or otherwise unavailable for fulfillment, we will automatically cancel the pre-order and issue a full refund to your original payment method within 5 business days. You will be notified by email with the reason for cancellation and the refund details. We do not substitute pre-ordered items with alternatives without your explicit consent.

---

**Q: I pre-ordered multiple items together. Can I cancel just the pre-order item and keep the rest?**
A: Yes, partial cancellations of orders containing both in-stock and pre-ordered items are supported. You can cancel only the pre-order item and the in-stock items will continue to be processed and shipped normally. In-stock items in a mixed order are typically shipped immediately, while pre-ordered items ship separately when available. Contact support to process a partial pre-order cancellation if the portal does not show the option.

---

## SECTION 6 — CUSTOM, PERSONALIZED, AND MADE-TO-ORDER CANCELLATIONS

---

**Q: Can I cancel a custom or personalized order?**
A: Custom and personalized orders (items engraved, monogrammed, printed-to-order, or built to your specifications) can only be cancelled within 2 hours of order placement, before production begins. Once our production team begins work on your order — which often happens within hours of order confirmation — cancellation is no longer possible. This is because the item has been created specifically for you and cannot be resold to another customer.

---

**Q: How do I know when production has started on my custom order?**
A: You will receive an email notification when your custom order moves from "Order Confirmed" to "In Production." This typically happens within 2–4 hours of order placement during business hours. The transition is also reflected in your account portal under "My Orders." Once "In Production" status is shown, cancellation is no longer available.

---

**Q: I made a mistake in my customization details. Can I correct it or cancel?**
A: If you notice a customization error (wrong name, date, design choice, etc.) immediately after placing the order, contact us within 1 hour via live chat with your order number. If production has not yet started, we will update the customization details at no charge. If production has already started, a correction may require cancelling and reordering, and a restocking or cancellation fee may apply to cover the cost of the work already completed on the original order.

---

**Q: What if my custom item arrives incorrectly made — not matching what I ordered?**
A: If the delivered custom item does not match the specifications you provided at checkout (wrong text, wrong design, wrong size), contact our support team within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the item and your original order confirmation. We will arrange a replacement at no charge, produced to the correct specifications. This is distinct from ordering the wrong specifications yourself — if the error is on our side, the remake is fully covered. If the error is in your submitted specifications, a discounted remake may be offered.

---

## SECTION 7 — SUBSCRIPTION ORDER CANCELLATIONS

---

**Q: Can I cancel a subscription order (recurring order) before it processes?**
A: Yes. Recurring subscription orders (e.g., monthly product boxes or replenishment orders) can be cancelled or skipped before the order processing date. Log in to your account, go to "Subscriptions" or "Scheduled Orders," and select "Skip" to delay a single shipment or "Cancel" to stop all future orders. Changes must be made at least 24 hours before the scheduled order date to take effect for that cycle.

---

**Q: What is the difference between skipping and cancelling a subscription order?**
A: Skipping a subscription order delays a single scheduled shipment without affecting future cycles. Your subscription remains active and the next scheduled order will be generated as usual. Cancelling a subscription order stops all future recurring orders permanently. If you only want to pause one shipment, use the "Skip" function. If you want to end the subscription entirely, use "Cancel."

---

**Q: I cancelled my subscription but a new order was still generated. Why?**
A: This can happen if you cancelled your subscription after the cutoff window for the next order cycle. Subscription orders are typically generated and locked 24–48 hours before the billing date. If your cancellation was submitted after the order was already generated and charged, the cancellation applies to all future cycles, but the already-generated order will still be fulfilled. Contact support if you believe this occurred in error and we will review whether a cancellation and refund of that order is possible.

---

**Q: Can I cancel a one-time add-on purchase made alongside my subscription?**
A: Yes. One-time add-on purchases linked to a subscription cycle are treated as regular orders and can be cancelled separately within the standard cancellation window (within 1 hour of order placement or before packing begins). Go to "My Orders," locate the add-on order, and use the standard cancellation process. Cancelling the add-on does not affect your subscription.

---

## SECTION 8 — BULK AND BUSINESS ORDER CANCELLATIONS

---

**Q: Can I cancel a bulk or wholesale order?**
A: Bulk and wholesale orders are subject to different cancellation terms than standard retail orders:
- Orders below $500: Standard 1-hour cancellation window applies.
- Orders between $500 and $5,000: Can be cancelled within 4 hours of placement, before fulfillment begins.
- Orders above $5,000: Cancellations must be requested within 24 hours of order placement, subject to review. A cancellation fee of 5–10% may apply if fulfillment has commenced.
Contact your account manager or our B2B support line for bulk order cancellations.

---

**Q: What if I need to cancel a purchase order (PO)-based order?**
A: Purchase order cancellations must be submitted in writing (email or formal written notice) to our B2B team at b2b@company.com. The PO cancellation request must include the original PO number, the reason for cancellation, and authorization from the appropriate signatory on the purchasing account. Cancellation timelines and any applicable fees are governed by the terms of the original purchase agreement or contract.

---

**Q: We placed a large corporate order and need to reduce the quantity. Is that possible?**
A: Quantity reductions on corporate or bulk orders are possible if requested before the order enters the fulfillment stage. Partial quantity reductions are treated as partial cancellations, and refunds are issued for the cancelled quantity. If the quantity reduction causes the order total to fall below a bulk discount threshold, the discount may be retroactively adjusted. Contact your account manager or B2B support for assistance.

## SECTION 9 — FRAUD-HELD AND FLAGGED ORDERS

---

**Q: My order was placed on a fraud hold. Can I cancel it?**
A: Orders placed on a fraud hold are temporarily locked while our security team verifies the transaction. You cannot cancel or modify a held order until the hold is resolved. Our team will contact you via email within 1–4 business hours to verify your identity and confirm the order. Once verified, the hold is lifted and the order proceeds normally. If the hold is not resolved within 48 hours, the order may be automatically cancelled and the payment released.

---

**Q: My order was cancelled by your system without my request. Why?**
A: Our system may automatically cancel an order for the following reasons:
- Payment verification failed or the payment was declined after initial authorization
- The order triggered our fraud detection system and could not be verified
- The item went out of stock after the order was placed and no substitute was available
- The shipping address could not be validated
- The order contained a pricing error that we were unable to honor
You will always be notified by email with the reason for the automatic cancellation. A full refund or void of the authorization will be processed immediately.

---

**Q: I believe my order was incorrectly flagged as fraudulent and cancelled. What can I do?**
A: If you believe your order was incorrectly cancelled due to a fraud flag, contact our support team with your order number and government-issued photo ID for identity verification. Our security team will review the case within 1 business day. If the cancellation is found to be in error, we will reinstate your order (if the item is still in stock) or issue a full refund and provide priority processing for a new order placement.

---

## SECTION 10 — CANCELLATION DURING PROMOTIONS AND SALES EVENTS

---

**Q: Can I cancel an order placed during a flash sale or limited-time promotion?**
A: Yes. Orders placed during flash sales or promotions follow the same cancellation rules as standard orders — you have the standard window to cancel. However, please note:
- If you cancel a sale-priced order and then wish to re-order, the promotional price may no longer be available if the sale has ended.
- Flash sale items often have accelerated fulfillment, which may shorten the effective cancellation window to 15–30 minutes.
- Promotionally priced items cancelled and re-ordered at full price are not eligible for a price adjustment back to the sale price after the promotion ends.

---

**Q: Can I cancel an order to take advantage of a better promotion that started after I placed my order?**
A: You may cancel an eligible order and re-order at the new promotional price, provided:
- The order is still within the cancellation window
- The promotional item is still available at the time of re-order
- The new promotion is valid for your account and region
Alternatively, if the promotion began within 14 days of your purchase, you may be eligible for a price-match adjustment without cancelling — contact support to check eligibility.

---

**Q: What happens if an item I ordered goes out of stock after I place the order but before it ships?**
A: If an item becomes unavailable after your order is placed, we will notify you by email as soon as possible. You will have the following options:
- Wait for restock: we will reserve your order and ship it as soon as the item is available again (we will provide an estimated restock date).
- Cancel and refund: we will cancel the out-of-stock item and issue a full refund for it. If it was the only item, the full order is cancelled.
- Substitute: if an equivalent alternative is available, we may suggest a substitute — you have the right to accept or decline it. Substitutes are never shipped without your explicit approval.

---

**Q: Can I cancel an order if the price drops after I purchase?**
A: A price drop after purchase does not automatically entitle you to a cancellation and refund. However, if the price drops within 14 days of your purchase and the item has not yet shipped, you may be eligible for a price match adjustment as a credit to your account — without needing to cancel. Contact support with your order number and evidence of the current lower price. If the item has already shipped, price match credits may still be offered as store credit.

---

## SECTION 11 — CANCELLATIONS AND ACCOUNT IMPACT

---

**Q: Does cancelling an order affect my account standing?**
A: Occasional order cancellations do not negatively affect your account. However, accounts with a very high ratio of cancellations relative to completed orders may be reviewed by our trust and safety team. This is to detect patterns of bad-faith ordering (e.g., placing orders to lock in stock or pricing with no intention to purchase). Legitimate cancellations — especially those due to delivery concerns, pricing errors, or changed circumstances — are never penalized.

---

**Q: Can my ability to place future orders be restricted due to repeated cancellations?**
A: Yes. If our system detects a pattern of excessive cancellations that appears abusive (e.g., placing and cancelling large numbers of orders during sale events, or repeatedly cancelling high-demand limited-edition items), your account may be flagged for review. In extreme cases, future purchases may require additional verification or be temporarily restricted. Our team will contact you before any restriction is applied to provide an opportunity to explain the activity.

---

**Q: Will my loyalty points or rewards be affected if I cancel an order?**
A: Yes. Loyalty points or rewards earned from a cancelled order will be deducted from your account balance when the cancellation is processed. If the points have already been applied to another purchase before the cancellation is processed, your account may show a negative points balance, which will be offset against future earned points. Points used during checkout that are returned as part of a cancellation refund will be credited back to your loyalty account.

---

**Q: If I cancel an order, does my referral credit get reversed?**
A: If a referral credit was applied to an order that is subsequently cancelled, the credit is returned to your account as store credit. If you referred someone and they cancel the order from which your referral credit was generated, the referral credit that was awarded to you is not revoked — it remains in your account.

---

## SECTION 12 — SPECIAL CIRCUMSTANCES

---

**Q: What if I need to cancel due to a medical emergency or bereavement?**
A: We understand that extraordinary circumstances arise. If you need to cancel an order that would otherwise be ineligible for cancellation due to a medical emergency, bereavement, or other serious personal circumstance, contact our support team and explain your situation. We handle such cases with compassion and discretion, and will do our best to accommodate a cancellation, return, or store credit arrangement even outside standard policy.

---

**Q: What if there is a natural disaster or emergency in my area that prevents delivery?**
A: If your delivery address is in an area affected by a natural disaster, extreme weather event, or civil emergency, contact our support team. We can hold the shipment at the carrier's facility, reroute to an alternate address, or cancel and refund the order if delivery cannot be guaranteed within a reasonable timeframe. We monitor carrier alerts and proactively reach out to customers in affected areas when large-scale disruptions are detected.

---

**Q: What if I accidentally placed a duplicate order?**
A: If you notice a duplicate order immediately after placing it, cancel the duplicate through the account portal or contact support. If both orders have shipped before you notice, you can return the duplicate order under our standard Return Policy. For identical duplicate orders placed within a few minutes of each other, our system may automatically flag and cancel one — you will be notified if this happens.

---

**Q: What if I ordered the wrong item entirely? Can I cancel and re-order?**
A: If you ordered the wrong item and the order is still within the cancellation window, cancel it through the portal and place a new order for the correct item. If the window has passed, you will need to accept delivery and initiate a return (see Return Policy). In some cases, if both the wrong and correct items are available and the order hasn't shipped, we can process an item swap without a full cancellation — contact support within 30 minutes of order placement to explore this option.

---

**Q: Can I cancel an order if I am dissatisfied with our customer service experience?**
A: Yes. If you wish to cancel an order due to a poor customer service experience, you are entitled to do so within the standard cancellation window. If the poor experience caused a delay in your ability to request a cancellation within the usual window, contact us and explain the situation — we will give your cancellation request fair consideration regardless of timing. We take all feedback about our service seriously and will flag your experience for internal review.
