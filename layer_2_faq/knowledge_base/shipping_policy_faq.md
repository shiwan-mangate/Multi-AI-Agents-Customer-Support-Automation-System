## SECTION 1 — GENERAL SHIPPING INFORMATION

---

**Q: What shipping carriers do you use?**
A: We partner with multiple carriers to ensure reliable and cost-effective delivery. Depending on your location, package size, and selected shipping method, your order may be shipped via:
- FedEx (Express and Ground)
- UPS (Standard, Express, and Overnight)
- USPS (First Class, Priority Mail, Priority Mail Express)
- DHL (for international shipments)
- Regional courier partners (for select local delivery zones)
The carrier is automatically assigned based on the most efficient route to your address. You can view the assigned carrier and tracking number in your order confirmation email.

---

**Q: Do you ship to P.O. Boxes?**
A: Yes, we ship to P.O. Boxes via USPS only. FedEx and UPS do not deliver to P.O. Boxes. If your order is assigned to FedEx or UPS and you have a P.O. Box address, our system will flag this and prompt you to provide a physical street address or switch to USPS shipping during checkout.

---

**Q: Do you ship to APO/FPO/DPO military addresses?**
A: Yes, we ship to APO (Army Post Office), FPO (Fleet Post Office), and DPO (Diplomatic Post Office) addresses via USPS Priority Mail. Delivery to military addresses can take 2–6 weeks depending on the deployment location. Expedited options are not available for military addresses.

---

**Q: Do you offer same-day delivery?**
A: Same-day delivery is available in select metropolitan areas for orders placed before 12:00 PM local time on business days. Eligible ZIP codes are shown during checkout. Same-day delivery is fulfilled via our local courier partners and carries an additional fee of $14.99–$24.99 depending on your location. Orders placed after the cutoff time will be scheduled for next-day delivery.

---

**Q: Can I ship to multiple addresses in a single order?**
A: At this time, each order can only be shipped to one address. If you need items sent to multiple addresses, please place separate orders for each destination. Our checkout allows you to save multiple addresses to your account for convenience when placing multiple orders.

---

**Q: Do you ship from multiple warehouses?**
A: Yes, we operate fulfillment centers in several locations. Your order may be split and shipped from multiple warehouses if items are available at different locations, which can result in separate tracking numbers and different delivery dates for items in the same order. You will be notified by email if your order is split-shipped.

---

## SECTION 2 — SHIPPING METHODS AND SPEEDS

---

**Q: What shipping options are available at checkout?**
A: We offer the following standard shipping options (availability may vary by region):
- **Standard Shipping:** 5–7 business days — Free on orders over $50, otherwise $5.99
- **Expedited Shipping:** 3–5 business days — $9.99
- **Express Shipping:** 2 business days — $14.99
- **Overnight Shipping:** Next business day by 10:30 AM — $29.99
- **Same-Day Delivery:** Available in select ZIP codes — $14.99–$24.99
All delivery timeframes are estimates from the date of shipment, not from the date of order placement.

---

**Q: What does "business day" mean for shipping?**
A: A business day is any Monday through Friday, excluding federal public holidays. Saturday and Sunday are not business days for shipping purposes, unless you selected a Saturday delivery option at checkout (available for select overnight and express services at an additional fee). Orders placed on a Friday after the cutoff time will begin processing on the next business day (Monday).

---

**Q: What is the order cut-off time for same-day processing?**
A: Orders placed by 2:00 PM local time (in the warehouse's time zone) on business days are processed and handed to the carrier the same day. Orders placed after 2:00 PM or on weekends/holidays will begin processing on the next business day. Processing time is separate from shipping transit time.

---

**Q: How long does order processing take before it ships?**
A: Standard processing time is 1–2 business days before the order is handed off to the carrier. During high-volume periods (sales events, holidays, new product launches), processing may take up to 3–4 business days. You will receive a shipping confirmation email with a tracking number as soon as your order has been dispatched from our warehouse.

---

**Q: Does the shipping speed I select guarantee delivery by a specific date?**
A: Shipping speeds are estimates based on carrier transit times and are not guaranteed delivery dates, except for Overnight Shipping (which carries a next-business-day commitment, subject to carrier guarantees). Delays due to weather, natural disasters, carrier capacity issues, or customs (for international) are outside our control and may affect estimated delivery dates.

---

**Q: Can I upgrade my shipping method after placing an order?**
A: You may request a shipping upgrade within 1 hour of placing your order, provided it has not yet entered the packing stage. Contact our support team immediately via live chat with your order number. If the order has already been packed or dispatched, a shipping upgrade cannot be applied. Any difference in shipping cost will be charged to your original payment method.

---

**Q: Do you offer weekend delivery?**
A: Saturday delivery is available for Overnight and Express shipments at an additional fee of $10.00–$15.00, depending on your location. Sunday delivery is not available at this time. Saturday delivery must be selected explicitly at checkout — standard shipping methods do not include weekend delivery.

## SECTION 3 — FREE SHIPPING

---

**Q: How do I qualify for free shipping?**
A: Free standard shipping is automatically applied to all orders with a subtotal of $50.00 or more (before taxes and after discounts). There is no promo code needed. If your cart total falls below $50, standard shipping will be charged at $5.99.

---

**Q: Does the free shipping threshold apply before or after discounts?**
A: The free shipping threshold is calculated based on the cart subtotal after discounts and promo codes are applied, but before taxes. For example, if your cart is $60 but you use a $15 coupon, your effective subtotal is $45, which falls below the free shipping threshold.

---

**Q: Can I get free shipping on expedited or express orders?**
A: Free shipping applies to Standard Shipping only. Expedited, Express, Overnight, and Same-Day delivery methods always carry their respective fees regardless of order value. Some promotional events may offer free expedited shipping; these are clearly communicated during the promotional period.

---

**Q: Does free shipping apply to international orders?**
A: Free shipping is available for domestic orders only. International orders are subject to calculated shipping fees based on destination, weight, and dimensions. Some international markets may qualify for subsidized shipping during promotional periods — check the website or contact support for current availability.

---

**Q: My order qualified for free shipping but I was charged. What should I do?**
A: If your order subtotal met the free shipping threshold and you were still charged for shipping, please contact our support team with your order number. This may occur due to a technical error or if an item in your cart is excluded from free shipping (e.g., oversized items, hazardous materials, or certain heavy goods). We will review and refund the shipping charge if applied in error.

---

## SECTION 4 — ORDER TRACKING

---

**Q: How do I track my order?**
A: Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a shipping confirmation email containing:
- Your tracking number
- The name of the carrier
- A direct link to the carrier's tracking page
You can also log in to your account, go to "Order History," and click "Track Order" for real-time status updates. Our tracking page consolidates information from all carriers in one place.

---

**Q: How long does it take for tracking information to become active?**
A: Tracking information typically becomes active within 12–24 hours of your order being handed off to the carrier. During peak periods, it may take up to 48 hours. If your tracking link shows "no information found" within this window, please wait and check again before contacting support.

---

**Q: My tracking says "delivered" but I didn't receive my package. What should I do?**
A: If the carrier marks your package as delivered but you have not received it:
1. Check all access points at your address — mailbox, porch, back door, with a neighbor, or at a building reception desk.
2. Wait 24 hours, as carriers occasionally update tracking prematurely.
3. Check with neighbors or anyone at the address who may have accepted the package.
4. If still not found after 24 hours, contact our support team. We will file a carrier investigation (this typically takes 3–5 business days). If the package is confirmed lost, we will send a replacement or issue a full refund.

---

**Q: My tracking hasn't updated in several days — is my package lost?**
A: Tracking updates can sometimes stall, especially during high-volume periods or when a package is in transit between carrier hubs. If your tracking has not updated in more than 5 business days, contact our support team. We will open a trace with the carrier and update you within 2 business days.

---

**Q: Can I redirect my package to a different address after it has shipped?**
A: Package redirection after shipment is possible with some carriers (FedEx and UPS) but is not guaranteed and may incur an additional fee of $10.00–$20.00. Contact our support team as soon as possible with your order number and new delivery address. We cannot redirect packages that are already out for delivery. USPS redirects must be managed directly through USPS Informed Delivery.

---

**Q: Do you offer package signature confirmation?**
A: Signature confirmation is automatically applied to high-value orders (above $200) and can be requested for any order at checkout for an additional $3.99. If signature confirmation is active, someone must be present at the delivery address to sign for the package. If no one is available, the carrier will leave a notice and attempt redelivery up to two more times before returning the package to us.

---

## SECTION 5 — DELIVERY ISSUES

---

**Q: What should I do if my package arrives damaged?**
A: If your package arrives visibly damaged on the outside:
1. If possible, note the damage with the carrier at the time of delivery.
2. Take photos of both the external packaging and the damaged contents.
3. Do not discard any packaging materials.
4. Contact our support team within 48 hours with your order number and photos.
We will arrange a replacement or full refund and file a damage claim with the carrier on your behalf.

---

**Q: My package was delivered to the wrong address. What happens now?**
A: If your package was delivered to an address other than yours:
1. Contact our support team immediately with your order number.
2. We will file a misdelivery report with the carrier.
3. The carrier will attempt to retrieve the package and deliver it to the correct address.
4. If retrieval is unsuccessful within 5 business days, we will send a replacement or issue a full refund.

---

**Q: My order is significantly delayed beyond the estimated delivery date. What can I do?**
A: If your order is delayed beyond the estimated window, please:
1. Check the tracking page for any carrier delay notifications.
2. Allow 2 additional business days beyond the estimate before contacting support (minor delays are common, especially in peak seasons).
3. If the delay exceeds 5 business days past the estimate with no resolution shown in tracking, contact our support team and we will investigate, offer a partial shipping refund where applicable, or escalate with the carrier.

---

**Q: What happens if I'm not home when delivery is attempted?**
A: The outcome depends on the carrier and whether signature confirmation is required:
- If no signature is required: the carrier will typically leave the package at the door, in a safe location, or in a mailbox.
- If signature is required: the carrier will leave a delivery attempt notice and try again on the next business day (up to 2 more attempts). After 3 failed attempts, the package is held at the carrier's local facility for up to 5 business days before being returned to us.

---

**Q: Can I schedule a specific delivery time?**
A: Specific delivery time slots are not available through standard shipping. However, FedEx Delivery Manager and UPS My Choice (free services offered directly by the carriers) allow you to set delivery windows, hold packages at a facility, or schedule redelivery. We recommend signing up for these free services using the tracking number from your shipping confirmation email.

---

**Q: What is a "safe place" delivery and how do I set one?**
A: A safe place delivery allows the carrier to leave your package in a pre-designated secure location (e.g., back porch, garage, with a neighbor) if you are not home. You can specify a safe place in your account settings under "Delivery Preferences" or add instructions in the order notes at checkout. Safe place instructions are passed to the carrier but cannot be guaranteed, as final delivery decisions are at the carrier's discretion.

---

**Q: The carrier attempted delivery but now says my package is being returned to sender. What do I do?**
A: If a package is being returned to us due to failed delivery attempts, address issues, or refusal at the door, contact our support team immediately. Once the package is returned to our warehouse and logged in (typically 5–10 business days after the return is initiated), we will:
- Reship the order to a corrected address at no additional shipping cost if the return was due to our error.
- Charge a reshipping fee if the return was due to an incorrect address provided by the customer.
- Issue a refund minus the original shipping cost if you prefer not to have it reshipped.

---

## SECTION 6 — INTERNATIONAL SHIPPING

---

**Q: Do you ship internationally?**
A: Yes, we ship to over 100 countries. International shipping availability and rates are shown at checkout once you enter your delivery address. Some products may be restricted from international shipping due to regulatory, safety, or export control reasons. If a product cannot be shipped to your country, you will be notified during checkout.

---

**Q: How are international shipping rates calculated?**
A: International shipping rates are calculated based on:
- Destination country and shipping zone
- Total package weight and dimensions (dimensional weight applies)
- Selected shipping speed (Standard, Expedited, or Express International)
- Any applicable carrier fuel surcharges
Rates are displayed in your cart currency during checkout. We use real-time carrier rate APIs to ensure accurate pricing.

---

**Q: How long does international shipping take?**
A: International delivery timeframes vary widely by destination:
- Canada and Mexico: 5–10 business days (Standard), 3–5 business days (Expedited)
- Europe (EU countries): 7–14 business days (Standard), 5–8 business days (Expedited)
- Asia-Pacific: 10–21 business days (Standard), 7–14 business days (Expedited)
- Rest of World: 14–30 business days (Standard), 10–20 business days (Expedited)
These estimates include customs clearance time, which can vary unpredictably.

---

**Q: Will my international order be subject to customs duties and import taxes?**
A: Yes. International shipments may be subject to customs duties, import taxes, and VAT/GST levied by your country's customs authority. These charges are the recipient's responsibility and are not included in our shipping fees or product prices. We have no control over these charges and cannot predict their amount. We recommend checking with your local customs office before ordering.

---

**Q: Do you offer Delivered Duty Paid (DDP) shipping to avoid customs surprises?**
A: DDP (Delivered Duty Paid) shipping is available for select countries, where all applicable duties and taxes are calculated and collected at checkout, so you pay a single total with no surprise fees upon delivery. Countries currently eligible for DDP are listed on our International Shipping page. For non-DDP countries, duties are billed by the carrier or customs agency at the time of delivery.

---

**Q: Can I mark my international order as a "gift" to avoid customs duties?**
A: No. We are required by law to accurately declare the contents and commercial value of all international shipments on customs documentation. Falsely marking commercial shipments as gifts to avoid customs duties is illegal and can result in the package being seized, returned, or destroyed by customs authorities. We do not accommodate requests to undervalue or misdeclare shipments.

---

**Q: My international package is stuck in customs — what should I do?**
A: Customs clearance delays are common and can range from a few days to several weeks depending on the destination country and volume of imports. While your package is in customs, tracking updates may pause. Steps to take:
1. Wait at least 10 business days from the last tracking update before escalating.
2. Contact your country's customs agency directly with the tracking number and any customs reference number provided.
3. Ensure any duties or taxes owed have been paid if your carrier has contacted you for payment.
4. If the package remains stuck beyond 30 days, contact our support team and we will assist with a carrier investigation.

---

**Q: Are there any countries you do not ship to?**
A: We are unable to ship to countries under international trade sanctions or embargoes, or countries where our carrier partners do not operate. Currently restricted destinations include (but may not be limited to): North Korea, Iran, Syria, Cuba, and the Crimea/Donbas regions. This list is subject to change based on current international regulations. You will be notified at checkout if we cannot ship to your country.

---

**Q: Do you provide a commercial invoice for international shipments?**
A: Yes, a commercial invoice is automatically generated and attached to all international shipments for customs purposes. If you require a copy of the commercial invoice for your records or for import purposes, you can download it from your order details page or request it from our support team.

## SECTION 7 — SHIPPING ADDRESS AND ORDER CHANGES

---

**Q: Can I change my shipping address after placing an order?**
A: Address changes are possible within 1 hour of order placement, before the order enters the packing stage. Contact our support team immediately via live chat with your order number and the corrected address. After packing has begun, address changes are generally not possible, though we can attempt a redirect with the carrier (see tracking section).

---

**Q: What happens if I enter the wrong shipping address?**
A: If you notice an address error:
- Contact us immediately — the sooner, the better. If the order hasn't shipped, we can update the address.
- If it has already shipped, we will attempt a carrier redirect (not guaranteed).
- If the package is delivered to the wrong address and cannot be retrieved, a replacement order may be shipped at the customer's expense, or a partial refund may be offered depending on the circumstances.
We are not responsible for delivery failures caused by incorrect addresses submitted by the customer.

---

**Q: Can I add delivery instructions to my order?**
A: Yes. During checkout, there is an "Delivery Instructions" or "Order Notes" field where you can leave instructions for the carrier — such as "Leave at back door," "Ring buzzer #4B," or "Do not leave with neighbor." These instructions are printed on the shipping label and passed to the carrier, but carriers are not always obligated to follow them, especially if they conflict with their standard delivery protocol.

---

**Q: Can I change the shipping method after placing an order?**
A: Shipping method changes are possible within 1 hour of placing your order if it has not yet entered processing. Contact live chat support immediately. If an upgrade results in a higher fee, the difference will be charged. If you downgrade to a lower-cost method, any difference will be refunded as store credit.

---

## SECTION 8 — SHIPPING FOR SPECIAL ITEMS

---

**Q: Are there items that cannot be shipped via standard methods?**
A: Yes. The following product categories are subject to special shipping requirements or restrictions:
- **Hazardous materials** (chemicals, flammable goods): require specialized carrier handling and may be restricted to ground shipping only
- **Oversized or heavy items** (over 70 lbs or exceeding dimensional limits): require freight or LTL shipping, with separate quotes
- **Perishable goods**: require refrigerated or expedited shipping and have restricted delivery zones
- **Lithium battery-containing products**: subject to IATA/IMDG regulations; cannot be shipped via certain air routes internationally
- **Alcohol and tobacco**: age verification and restricted shipping zones apply
- **Firearms and regulated items**: shipped only where legally permitted, with required documentation

---

**Q: Do you offer freight or LTL shipping for large orders?**
A: Yes, for orders that are too large or heavy for standard parcel shipping, we offer freight and Less-Than-Truckload (LTL) shipping. Freight quotes are provided separately and are not shown during standard checkout. Contact our B2B or enterprise support line to request a freight quote. LTL shipments typically include liftgate service and delivery to a business address; residential freight delivery requires advance scheduling.

---

**Q: How do you ship fragile or high-value items?**
A: Fragile and high-value items receive enhanced packaging, which may include double-boxing, foam inserts, air pillows, or custom-fitted inserts. High-value orders (above $200) automatically include signature confirmation and may be shipped with declared value coverage (shipping insurance). See the Shipping Insurance section below for details.

---

**Q: Do you ship perishable items?**
A: Yes, select perishable products are shipped using insulated packaging with ice packs or dry ice, and are dispatched Monday through Wednesday only to ensure arrival before the weekend (to minimize the risk of packages sitting undelivered over the weekend). Perishable items can only be shipped to certain regions and always via Expedited or Express shipping. No standard shipping option is available for perishables.

---

## SECTION 9 — SHIPPING INSURANCE AND DECLARED VALUE

---

**Q: Is my shipment insured?**
A: All shipments include basic carrier liability coverage:
- USPS: Up to $100 for Priority Mail; up to $50 for other services
- FedEx/UPS: Up to $100 included; additional declared value available
For orders valued above the carrier's basic liability, we automatically purchase additional carrier-declared value coverage for orders over $200. For high-value orders above $1,000, third-party shipping insurance is applied.

---

**Q: Can I purchase additional shipping insurance?**
A: Yes, you can add shipping insurance at checkout for a fee of approximately 1.5% of the declared value (minimum $2.99). This covers the full replacement value of your order in the event of loss, damage, or theft during transit. Insurance claims are processed through our support team, who will coordinate with the insurer on your behalf.

---

**Q: How do I file a shipping insurance claim?**
A: To file a shipping insurance claim:
1. Contact our support team within 7 days of the delivery date (or the estimated delivery date, in case of loss).
2. Provide your order number, a description of the loss or damage, and supporting photos if applicable.
3. Our team will file the claim with the carrier or insurer.
4. Claims are typically resolved within 7–14 business days. You will be reimbursed via the original payment method or store credit.

---

## SECTION 10 — SHIPPING COSTS AND INVOICING

---

**Q: Are shipping costs shown before I complete my purchase?**
A: Yes. Shipping costs are calculated and displayed at checkout before you confirm payment. You will see an itemized breakdown including the product subtotal, applicable shipping fee, taxes, and any discounts applied. Shipping costs are final at the time of checkout and are not subject to retroactive changes unless an error occurred.

---

**Q: Why did my shipping cost change between adding to cart and checkout?**
A: Shipping costs in the cart preview are estimated based on your default address (if saved). Final shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on the exact delivery address, selected shipping method, package weight, and any applicable promotions. The final checkout figure is always the accurate amount.

---

**Q: Are shipping fees taxable?**
A: Shipping fees are subject to sales tax in certain states and jurisdictions in accordance with local tax law. Whether shipping is taxable is determined by your delivery address and the applicable tax regulations, which are automatically applied during checkout. We comply with all applicable tax laws and remit collected taxes to the appropriate authorities.

---

**Q: Can I get a receipt or invoice for shipping charges?**
A: Yes. A full order invoice including itemized shipping charges is included in your order confirmation email and is also available to download from your account's "Order History" page. Business customers who require formal invoices with VAT or GST breakdowns can request these from our support team.

---

## SECTION 11 — SHIPPING DURING PEAK AND PROMOTIONAL PERIODS

---

**Q: Are there shipping delays during holidays or sale events?**
A: Yes. During high-volume periods (Black Friday, Cyber Monday, Christmas, New Year, and major promotional events), processing times may increase by 2–4 business days, and carriers may experience delays due to surge volume. We display banner notifications on our website during such periods with updated processing estimates. We recommend ordering at least 10–14 business days before a critical date during peak season.

---

**Q: When is the last day I can order to guarantee delivery by Christmas (or another holiday)?**
A: We publish holiday shipping cutoff dates each year on our website and via email newsletter in November. As a general guide:
- Standard Shipping: Order by December 14
- Expedited Shipping: Order by December 18
- Express Shipping: Order by December 20
- Overnight Shipping: Order by December 23 (for December 24 delivery)
These dates are estimates and may vary by destination and carrier capacity.

---

**Q: Does your warehouse close during public holidays?**
A: Our warehouses are closed on the following U.S. federal public holidays: New Year's Day, Memorial Day, Independence Day (July 4), Labor Day, Thanksgiving Day, and Christmas Day. Orders placed on these days will begin processing on the next business day. Customer support availability on holidays is limited to email and chatbot; live agents return the following business day.

## SECTION 12 — CLICK & COLLECT / IN-STORE PICKUP

---

**Q: Can I pick up my order in store instead of having it shipped?**
A: Yes, in-store pickup (Click & Collect) is available at select retail locations. During checkout, select "Pick Up In Store" and choose your preferred location from the list of participating stores. You will receive a "Ready for Pickup" email once your order is prepared, typically within 2–4 hours on business days.

---

**Q: What do I need to bring to pick up my order?**
A: To collect your order in store, bring:
- Your "Ready for Pickup" email or order confirmation (printed or on your phone)
- A valid government-issued photo ID
- The payment card used at checkout (for verification on high-value orders)
A designated team member at the pickup counter will verify your identity and release the order to you.

---

**Q: How long does the store hold my Click & Collect order?**
A: Your order will be held in store for 7 calendar days from the "Ready for Pickup" notification. If uncollected after 7 days, the order will be cancelled and a full refund will be issued to your original payment method within 5–7 business days.

---

**Q: Can someone else pick up my Click & Collect order on my behalf?**
A: Yes, you can authorize another person to collect your order. To do so, contact our support team before the pickup and provide the name of the authorized person. That person must present their own valid photo ID and your order confirmation number at the store. Unauthorized third-party collections will be refused.

## SECTION 13 — SUSTAINABILITY AND PACKAGING

---

**Q: What kind of packaging do you use?**
A: We are committed to sustainable packaging practices. Our packaging includes:
- Recycled and recyclable cardboard boxes
- Biodegradable or paper-based void fill (replacing Styrofoam peanuts)
- Water-activated kraft paper tape (plastic-free)
- Soy-based inks for printed materials
- Right-sized packaging to minimize waste and reduce dimensional weight charges

---

**Q: Can I request minimal packaging or eco-friendly packaging?**
A: We use eco-friendly packaging as our default. If you would like your order packaged with minimal materials (e.g., no extra inserts or promotional materials), you can select the "Minimal Packaging" option at checkout where available, or note it in the delivery instructions. We are working toward fully eliminating single-use plastics from our supply chain by 2026.

---

**Q: Do you offset the carbon footprint of shipping?**
A: Yes. We participate in a certified carbon offset program that neutralizes the estimated carbon emissions of every shipment. A portion of each shipping fee is allocated to verified reforestation and renewable energy projects. You can read more about our carbon-neutral shipping commitment on our Sustainability page.

---

**Q: How should I dispose of or recycle the packaging materials?**
A: Our cardboard boxes, paper-based void fill, and kraft tape can be placed in standard curbside recycling bins. Bubble wrap or any plastic film, if included (typically only for fragile items), should be taken to a designated plastic film recycling drop-off location (many grocery stores accept these). We include a recycling guide insert in most packages.
