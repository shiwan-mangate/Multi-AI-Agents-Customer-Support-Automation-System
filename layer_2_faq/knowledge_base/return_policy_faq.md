## SECTION 1 — GENERAL RETURN ELIGIBILITY

---

**Q: What is your general return policy?**
A: We accept returns on most items within 30 days of the delivery date. To be eligible for a return, the item must be in its original condition — unused, unwashed, unaltered, and in its original packaging with all tags, accessories, manuals, and included components intact. Items that do not meet these conditions may be refused or subject to a restocking fee. Once a return is received and inspected, a refund or exchange will be processed based on the outcome of the inspection.

---

**Q: How do I know if my item is eligible for a return?**
A: An item is eligible for return if all of the following are true:
- It was purchased directly from us (our website, app, or physical store)
- The return is initiated within 30 days of the delivery date
- The item is in original, unused condition with all original packaging and accessories
- The item is not in a non-returnable category (see the exclusions section)
If you are unsure, contact our support team with your order number and item description and we will confirm eligibility before you initiate a return.

---

**Q: What items cannot be returned?**
A: The following items are non-returnable under any circumstances:
- Perishable goods (food, beverages, flowers, live plants)
- Hazardous materials, flammable liquids, or compressed gases
- Personal hygiene and intimate items (underwear, swimwear, earrings, cosmetics) if opened or used
- Digital downloads and software licenses once activated or downloaded
- Gift cards and store credit vouchers
- Custom-made, personalized, or made-to-order items
- Items marked as "Final Sale" at the time of purchase
- Prescription medical devices or pharmaceuticals
- Opened consumable items (printer ink, batteries, health supplements, single-use items)
- Mattresses, pillows, and bedding products after removal from original sealed packaging

---

**Q: Can I return an item that was a gift?**
A: Yes. Gift recipients can return eligible items for store credit equal to the current selling price of the item. A gift receipt or the order number from the original purchase is required. If you do not have either, contact support with as many order details as possible and we will attempt to locate the original transaction. Cash or card refunds for gift returns are only issued to the original purchaser's payment method.

---

**Q: Can I return an item I purchased from a third-party retailer?**
A: No. Items purchased from third-party retailers (Amazon, Walmart, Target, department stores, or unauthorized resellers) must be returned through that retailer according to their own return policy. We cannot accept returns, process refunds, or exchange products purchased outside our official channels. Always check the retailer's return policy before purchasing.

---

**Q: Can I return a product if I simply changed my mind?**
A: Yes, change-of-mind returns are accepted within 30 days, provided the item is unused and in its original, resalable condition with all original packaging. Change-of-mind returns for physical products are subject to the customer covering return shipping costs (unless a prepaid label promotion is active). For digital goods and services that have been accessed, change-of-mind returns are not accepted.

---

**Q: What if I bought the wrong size or color?**
A: Incorrect size or color purchases are treated as change-of-mind returns and are eligible for return within 30 days in original, unused condition. You may request an exchange for the correct size or color (subject to availability) or a refund. For exchanges, we will ship the replacement once the original item is received and inspected at our warehouse. If the replacement item is at a different price point, the difference will be charged or refunded accordingly.

---

**Q: Is there a minimum or maximum order value for returns?**
A: There is no minimum order value required to initiate a return. For high-value returns (above $500), additional identity verification may be required before the return is approved, and a senior support agent will handle the case. There is no maximum order value for returns, but exceptionally high-value returns may require additional documentation such as proof of purchase and photos of the item.

## SECTION 2 — HOW TO INITIATE A RETURN

---

**Q: How do I start a return?**
A: To initiate a return:
1. Log in to your account on our website or app.
2. Navigate to "Order History" and select the relevant order.
3. Click "Return Item(s)" and select the item(s) you wish to return.
4. Choose your reason for return from the dropdown menu.
5. Select your preferred resolution: refund, exchange, or store credit.
6. Submit the return request.
You will receive a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number and a prepaid return shipping label (where applicable) within 1–2 business days via email. Returns without a valid RMA number may be refused at our warehouse.

---

**Q: Can I return an item without creating an online account?**
A: Yes. If you checked out as a guest, you can initiate a return by:
- Visiting our Returns Portal at returns.company.com and entering your order number and email address.
- Contacting our support team via email, live chat, or phone with your order number and purchase details.
We will look up your order and generate the return authorization for you.

---

**Q: What information do I need to provide when initiating a return?**
A: You will need:
- Order number or invoice number
- Email address used at the time of purchase
- Item(s) being returned and the quantity
- Reason for return (e.g., defective, wrong item, change of mind, not as described)
- Preferred resolution (refund, exchange, or store credit)
- Photos of the item and packaging if the return is due to damage or defect

---

**Q: Can I return multiple items from the same order in one return request?**
A: Yes. When submitting a return request, you can select multiple items from the same order to include in a single return. Each item will be assessed individually during inspection. If items are from different orders, you must submit separate return requests for each order, even if you ship them together (include separate RMA numbers on each item's packaging inside the box).

---

**Q: Can I return items from multiple orders in one shipment?**
A: You may physically ship items from multiple orders in one box, but you must include a separate RMA number and return form for each order inside the package. Clearly label each item with its corresponding RMA number. Failure to include the correct RMA documentation may delay processing. Each order's refund or exchange will be handled separately.

---

**Q: How long does it take to receive my RMA and return label after submitting a return request?**
A: RMA numbers and return labels are typically issued within 1–2 business days of submitting your return request. During peak periods (holiday season, major sales events), it may take up to 3–4 business days. If you have not received your RMA after 3 business days, check your spam/junk folder before contacting support.

---

**Q: My return request was denied. Why?**
A: A return request may be denied for the following reasons:
- The item is in a non-returnable category
- The 30-day return window has expired
- The item shows signs of use, damage, or tampering not consistent with a defect claim
- The item is incomplete (missing parts, accessories, or original packaging)
- The account has been flagged for repeated return abuse
If you believe your return was incorrectly denied, contact our support team and request a review. Provide any additional evidence (photos, receipts, correspondence) to support your case.

## SECTION 3 — RETURN WINDOW AND DEADLINES

---

**Q: When does the 30-day return window start?**
A: The 30-day return window begins on the date your order is delivered — specifically, the date recorded by the carrier in the tracking system as "Delivered." For in-store purchases, the return window begins on the date of the in-store transaction as shown on your receipt.

---

**Q: What if I didn't open or use the item until after the 30-day window?**
A: The return window is based on the delivery date, not the date you first opened the item. If you open an item after 30 days and find it defective or not as described, please contact us — warranty claims (typically valid for 1 year from purchase) may still apply even if the general return window has passed. We handle post-window issues on a case-by-case basis.

---

**Q: What if the return window falls on a weekend or holiday?**
A: If the final day of your 30-day return window falls on a Saturday, Sunday, or a public holiday, the deadline is extended to the next business day. For example, if day 30 is a Sunday, you have until the following Monday to submit your return request.

---

**Q: Can I return an item after 30 days?**
A: Late returns (after 30 days) are evaluated on a case-by-case basis at our discretion. We may offer:
- Store credit (rather than a cash refund) for items returned 31–60 days after delivery if the item is in original condition
- A warranty replacement or repair for defective items within the warranty period (up to 1 year)
- No return option for items returned beyond 60 days, unless covered by an extended warranty
Contact our support team to discuss your situation before shipping anything back.

---

**Q: Do you offer extended return windows during the holiday season?**
A: Yes. Items purchased between November 1 and December 31 are eligible for an extended return window through January 31 of the following year. This allows gift recipients additional time to return or exchange items received during the holiday season. The extended window applies to all eligible product categories and follows the same return conditions as our standard policy.

---

**Q: I received my order late due to a shipping delay. Does the return window still start on the original delivery date?**
A: Yes, the return window starts on the actual delivery date (as confirmed by carrier tracking), not the estimated delivery date. If your order arrived significantly late due to our error or a carrier failure, contact support — we will manually extend your return window to ensure you receive the full 30 days from the date you actually received the item.

## SECTION 4 — RETURN SHIPPING

---

**Q: Who pays for return shipping?**
A: Return shipping responsibility depends on the reason for the return:

**We cover return shipping (free prepaid label) when:**
- The item arrived damaged or defective
- The wrong item was sent
- The item does not match the product description on our website
- The return is due to our fulfillment error

**The customer covers return shipping when:**
- Returning due to change of mind
- Ordered the wrong size, color, or variant
- The item is no longer wanted
- The item was a gift and the recipient wishes to return it

---

**Q: How do I get a prepaid return shipping label?**
A: Once your return request is approved and a prepaid label is applicable, it will be emailed to you as a PDF attachment within 1–2 business days. You can also download it directly from your account portal under "Order History" → "View Return." Print the label and affix it securely to the outside of your return package, covering any existing shipping labels.

---

**Q: What if I don't have a printer for the prepaid return label?**
A: If you do not have access to a printer, you can:
1. Visit a carrier location (FedEx Office, UPS Store) — show the label on your phone and they will print it for you, usually at no charge.
2. Use a QR code return option: certain carriers allow you to present a QR code at the drop-off location and they will print and apply the label for you. If this option is available for your return, a QR code will be included alongside the PDF label in your return email.

---

**Q: Can I use my own shipping carrier for returns?**
A: Yes, you can use your own preferred carrier if you are responsible for return shipping costs. We recommend using a carrier that provides tracking and delivery confirmation, as we cannot process your return or refund until the item is confirmed received at our warehouse. We are not responsible for packages lost in transit when using a customer-arranged carrier without tracking.

---

**Q: What carrier should I use for self-arranged return shipping?**
A: We accept returns via any major carrier (FedEx, UPS, USPS, DHL). For domestic returns, USPS Priority Mail or UPS Ground offer a good balance of cost and reliability. Always request a tracking number and retain your drop-off receipt until your refund or exchange has been confirmed. Ship returns to the address provided in your RMA confirmation email — do not ship to our corporate headquarters or retail stores unless specified.

---

**Q: Can I drop off my return at one of your retail stores?**
A: Yes, eligible returns can be dropped off at any of our company-operated retail store locations. Bring the item in its original packaging along with your order confirmation and a valid photo ID. Store associates will inspect the item, issue you a return receipt, and log the return. Refunds for in-store drop-offs are processed after warehouse inspection, not at the point of drop-off.

---

**Q: How should I package my return item?**
A: Pack the return item securely to prevent damage during transit:
- Use the original product box if available, placed inside a plain outer shipping box.
- If the original box is not available, use a sturdy corrugated box of appropriate size.
- Wrap fragile items with bubble wrap or padding to prevent movement.
- Include all original accessories, manuals, cables, and packaging inserts.
- Place the completed return form or a copy of your RMA confirmation inside the package.
- Affix the return label clearly on the outside, covering any previous shipping labels.
Items that arrive at our warehouse damaged due to inadequate return packaging may result in a reduced refund or rejection of the return.

---

**Q: Is there a weight or size limit on return packages?**
A: Standard return shipping labels provided by us support packages up to 70 lbs and standard parcel dimensions. For oversized or extremely heavy returns (furniture, large appliances, bulky equipment), a special return process applies — contact our support team to arrange freight return pickup. Do not attempt to ship oversized items with a standard return label, as the carrier may refuse the package.

---

## SECTION 5 — RETURN INSPECTION AND APPROVAL

---

**Q: What happens after I ship my return?**
A: After you ship your return:
1. The package is in transit to our returns processing warehouse (1–7 business days depending on your location).
2. Upon arrival, our returns team logs the package and begins inspection (1–3 business days).
3. You will receive an email notifying you that your return has been received.
4. After inspection, you will receive a second email confirming the outcome: approved, partially approved, or rejected, along with the refund or exchange details.

---

**Q: What does the return inspection involve?**
A: Our returns team inspects every returned item for:
- Completeness: all original components, accessories, manuals, and packaging are present
- Condition: the item is free of damage, signs of use, alterations, or tampering beyond what was claimed
- Authenticity: the returned item matches the item originally shipped (same serial number or product ID where applicable)
- Match to claim: the condition of the item is consistent with the stated return reason (e.g., a "defective" claim is verified by testing the item)

---

**Q: How long does return inspection take?**
A: Standard return inspection takes 1–3 business days after the package is received at our warehouse. During peak periods (holiday season, major sales events), inspection may take up to 5–7 business days. You will be notified by email at each stage: receipt, inspection start, and resolution.

---

**Q: What if my return is rejected after inspection?**
A: If a return is rejected after inspection (e.g., item is used, damaged beyond the stated reason, or incomplete), you will receive an email explaining the specific reason for rejection. Options at that point include:
- Accepting the item back (we will reship it to you at your cost)
- Appealing the decision by contacting our support team with additional evidence within 7 business days
- In some cases, a partial refund may be offered depending on the item's resalable condition

---

**Q: Can I track my return shipment after I drop it off?**
A: Yes. If you used our prepaid return label, the label includes a tracking number that you can use on the carrier's website or our order tracking page. If you used your own carrier, use the tracking number provided by that carrier. We recommend bookmarking or saving the tracking number until your refund or exchange is fully processed.

---

**Q: What happens if my return package is lost in transit?**
A: If you used our prepaid return label, we assume responsibility for the package once it is scanned by the carrier at drop-off. If the package is lost in transit, contact our support team with your drop-off receipt (proof that you handed the package to the carrier). We will file a carrier claim and process your refund or exchange once the investigation is complete (typically 5–10 business days).

If you used your own shipping method without tracking, we cannot process a refund for items we do not receive confirmation of. Always use tracked shipping for self-arranged returns.

---

## SECTION 6 — EXCHANGES

---

**Q: Can I exchange an item instead of getting a refund?**
A: Yes, exchanges are available for eligible items within the 30-day return window. When submitting your return request, select "Exchange" as your preferred resolution and specify the replacement item (size, color, variant). Exchanges are subject to product availability. If the replacement item is out of stock, we will offer a refund or notify you when it becomes available.

---

**Q: How does the exchange process work?**
A: The exchange process works as follows:
1. Submit a return request and select "Exchange."
2. Specify the replacement item details.
3. Ship the original item back using the provided return label.
4. Once we receive and inspect the original item, the replacement is dispatched.
5. You will receive a new shipping confirmation for the replacement item.
Exchange processing typically takes 5–10 business days from the date we receive your return.

---

**Q: Do I have to wait for my return to be received before the exchange is sent?**
A: In most cases, yes — we dispatch the replacement after receiving and inspecting the returned item. However, for verified customers with a strong account history and for high-confidence return reasons (e.g., wrong item shipped), we may offer an advance exchange where the replacement is sent immediately. Contact support to discuss whether advance exchange is available for your situation.

---

**Q: What if the item I want to exchange for is more or less expensive?**
A: If the exchange item is more expensive, you will be charged the price difference to your original payment method before the replacement is shipped. If the exchange item is less expensive, the price difference will be refunded to your original payment method or as store credit, based on your preference. Exchanges for items of equal value incur no additional charge.

---

**Q: Can I exchange a product for a completely different product, not just a different variant?**
A: Exchanging for a completely different product (a different product category or item, not just a size or color variant) is processed as a return and a new purchase. Submit a standard return for the original item and place a new order for the desired product. If you would like to use the refund value as store credit toward the new purchase, let us know and we can coordinate this to minimize wait time.

---

**Q: Can I exchange a gift I received?**
A: Yes, gift exchanges are handled the same way as standard exchanges. The exchange value is based on the current selling price of the item. If you do not have the original order number, contact support with as many details as possible about the purchase. Replacement items for gift exchanges are shipped to the address you provide, and no information about the transaction is communicated to the original purchaser unless you request it.

---

## SECTION 7 — CONDITION REQUIREMENTS AND RESTOCKING FEES

---

**Q: What counts as "original condition" for a return?**
A: "Original condition" means the item is:
- Unused and unworn (for apparel, footwear, or wearable accessories)
- Unassembled, or re-assembled to original state if partially assembled
- Free of odors, stains, dirt, pet hair, or cosmetic damage
- Complete with all original tags, labels, and stickers still attached
- Accompanied by all original packaging, inserts, manuals, warranty cards, and accessories
- Not altered, modified, repaired, or tampered with

---

**Q: Can I return an item I tried on or briefly tested?**
A: Items that have been briefly tried on (apparel) or lightly tested (electronics) in a manner consistent with evaluating the product for suitability may still be accepted for return, provided there are no signs of actual use, damage, or wear. For example, trying on a jacket to check the fit is acceptable; wearing it outdoors is not. Testing a device by powering it on and checking features is acceptable; installing software or modifying settings extensively may not be.

---

**Q: Will I be charged a restocking fee?**
A: A restocking fee applies in the following scenarios:

| Scenario | Restocking Fee |
|---|---|
| Unopened, original condition | No fee |
| Opened but unused, all packaging present | No fee |
| Opened and lightly used, resalable condition | 15% of item price |
| Missing accessories, manuals, or partial packaging | 20% of item price |
| Item shows signs of heavy use or cosmetic wear | Up to 25% of item price |
| Defective or damaged by us (our error) | No fee (full refund) |

Restocking fees are waived for all defective product returns, wrong item shipments, and returns made within 7 days of delivery.

---

**Q: What happens if I return an item without its original packaging?**
A: Missing original packaging may result in:
- A restocking fee of 15–20% if the item is otherwise resalable
- Refusal of the return if the packaging is required for the item to be resold (e.g., sealed software, sealed collectibles, or items where the box is part of the product's value)
We encourage customers to retain original packaging for at least 30 days after purchase.

---

**Q: What if tags have been removed from apparel?**
A: Apparel with removed hang tags may still be accepted for return if the item is otherwise unworn and in original condition. However, removal of sewn-in care/brand labels may indicate the garment has been worn and may result in the return being rejected or subject to a restocking fee. When in doubt, do not remove tags until you are certain you want to keep the item.

---

**Q: Can I return an item that has been assembled?**
A: Items that require assembly (furniture, exercise equipment, flat-pack items) can be returned if they are fully disassembled back to their original state and all parts, hardware, and instructions are included. Items that cannot be disassembled without causing damage, or that have been structurally modified, are not returnable. Contact support before attempting to disassemble and return large items.

## SECTION 8 — DEFECTIVE AND WARRANTY RETURNS

---

**Q: What should I do if I receive a defective product?**
A: If you receive a defective product:
1. Document the defect with photos or a short video clearly showing the issue.
2. Contact our support team within 48 hours of delivery (or as soon as the defect is discovered for defects that appear during use).
3. Provide your order number, a description of the defect, and attach the supporting photos or video.
4. Our team will assess the claim and offer a replacement, repair, or full refund — your choice.
For defects that appear after the initial return window but within the warranty period, see the warranty section below.

---

**Q: What is covered under your product warranty?**
A: Our standard product warranty covers manufacturing defects and failures under normal use for 1 year from the date of purchase. Warranty coverage includes:
- Structural failures not caused by misuse or physical damage
- Electronic or mechanical malfunctions under normal operating conditions
- Premature material deterioration not caused by wear and tear or environmental factors

Warranty does not cover:
- Physical damage (drops, cracks, spills, impact)
- Normal wear and tear
- Damage caused by misuse, modification, or unauthorized repair
- Cosmetic damage that does not affect functionality
- Damage from use outside product specifications (e.g., incorrect voltage)

---

**Q: How do I make a warranty claim?**
A: To make a warranty claim:
1. Contact our warranty support team at warranty@company.com or via the "Warranty Claim" option in your account portal.
2. Provide your order number, purchase date, a description of the defect, and supporting photos or video.
3. Our warranty team will assess the claim within 3 business days and issue a decision.
4. If approved, we will provide a prepaid return label for the defective item and dispatch a replacement or offer a repair.
5. If a repair is chosen, turnaround time is typically 7–14 business days once the item is received at our repair facility.

---

**Q: What if my product becomes defective after the 1-year warranty period?**
A: Post-warranty defects are not covered under the standard warranty. However, we encourage you to contact support as we sometimes offer out-of-warranty repair services at a discounted rate, or extended warranty programs for select product categories. Additionally, in some jurisdictions, consumer protection laws provide statutory warranty rights beyond our stated warranty period — we comply with all applicable local laws.

---

**Q: Do you offer extended warranties?**
A: Yes, extended warranty plans are available for select product categories at the time of purchase. Extended warranties extend coverage to 2 or 3 years from the purchase date and may include additional benefits such as accidental damage protection, priority support, and a dedicated warranty helpline. Extended warranty terms and pricing are shown on the product page and at checkout for eligible items.

---

**Q: I bought an extended warranty. How do I make a claim under it?**
A: Extended warranty claims follow the same process as standard warranty claims. When contacting our warranty team, mention that you have an extended warranty plan and provide your extended warranty policy number (found in your original purchase confirmation email). Extended warranty claims are handled by our dedicated warranty team and may have different resolution timelines depending on the plan terms.

---

## SECTION 9 — IN-STORE RETURNS

---

**Q: Can I return an online purchase to a physical store?**
A: Yes, items purchased online can be returned to any of our company-operated retail store locations. Bring the item in its original packaging, your order confirmation email or packing slip, and a valid photo ID. The store associate will process the return and issue a return receipt. Refunds for online purchases returned in-store are processed centrally after the item is logged, and may take 3–5 business days to appear on your original payment method.

---

**Q: Can I return an in-store purchase by mail?**
A: Yes, in-store purchases can be returned by mail within the 30-day return window. Visit our Returns Portal, enter your receipt number and the email address used at purchase, and follow the process to generate a return label. In-store purchase receipts include a barcode that can be scanned at the portal for quick lookup.

---

**Q: Are the return policies the same for in-store and online purchases?**
A: Yes, the same 30-day return window, condition requirements, and eligibility criteria apply to both in-store and online purchases. The main difference is that in-store purchases returned in-store may receive an immediate exchange or store credit (cash refunds still require processing time), whereas online returns always require warehouse inspection before a refund is issued.

---

**Q: Can I get an immediate refund when returning in-store?**
A: Immediate exchanges and store credit are available in-store for eligible returns. Cash or card refunds, however, are not issued at the point-of-sale terminal for returned items — they are processed centrally after the item is inspected and logged. The refund will appear on your original payment method within 3–5 business days. You will receive a return receipt confirming the return was accepted.

---

**Q: What if there is no store near me?**
A: If you do not have access to a nearby store, use our online return process to ship the item back. Our Returns Portal is available 24/7 and guides you through the process step by step. If you need personalized assistance, our support team can help you initiate a return and arrange a carrier pickup from your home or office in select regions.

---

## SECTION 10 — RETURNS FOR SPECIAL PURCHASE TYPES

---

**Q: Can I return a subscription box or curated kit?**
A: Subscription boxes and curated kits can be returned within 30 days if the box is unopened and sealed. Once the subscription box has been opened, individual items within the box may be eligible for return if they meet the standard return conditions. Items in subscription boxes that are damaged or defective on arrival are always eligible for a replacement or refund regardless of whether the box was opened.

---

**Q: Can I return an item purchased with a buy-now-pay-later (BNPL) service?**
A: Yes. Returns on BNPL purchases (Klarna, Afterpay, Affirm, etc.) follow the same return policy. Once your return is approved and the refund is processed on our end, we will notify the BNPL provider of the refund. The provider will then adjust your outstanding installments or credit the amount back to your account according to their own refund timeline (typically 3–10 business days). Contact the BNPL provider directly if you have questions about how the credit will be applied to your repayment plan.

---

**Q: Can I return an item purchased with store credit or a gift card?**
A: Yes. Items purchased with store credit or a gift card are eligible for return under the standard policy. Refunds for these purchases are issued as store credit back to your account. If your purchase was split between a gift card and a credit/debit card, the refund is applied proportionally — store credit first, then the remainder to your card.

---

**Q: Can I return a pre-order item?**
A: Pre-orders can be cancelled for a full refund at any time before the item ships. Once a pre-order has shipped, the standard 30-day return policy applies from the delivery date. If a pre-ordered item is significantly delayed beyond the original estimated ship date, you may cancel the pre-order and receive a full refund regardless of the standard policy window.

---

**Q: What is the return policy for limited edition or collector's items?**
A: Limited edition and collector's items follow the standard return policy, with the additional condition that the item must be returned in completely sealed, unopened condition to be eligible for a full refund. Once a limited edition or collector's item is opened, it may only be eligible for return if it is defective. This is because opened limited edition items lose significant resale value. Check the product page for any item-specific return notes.

---

**Q: Can I return a product purchased during a flash sale or special event?**
A: Yes, flash sale and special event purchases follow the standard return policy. However, refunds for sale items reflect the sale price paid, not the full retail price. If the item was marked "Final Sale" at the time of purchase, it is not eligible for return under any circumstances.

---

## SECTION 11 — INTERNATIONAL RETURNS

---

**Q: Can I return an international order?**
A: Yes, international returns are accepted under the same 30-day return policy. However, international customers are responsible for return shipping costs (unless the return is due to a defect or our error). We recommend using a tracked international shipping service and declaring the package as a "Return of Goods" on customs documentation to avoid re-import duties on the return package.

---

**Q: Who pays customs duties on international returns?**
A: Customs duties and import taxes originally paid by the customer on the inbound shipment are non-refundable by us. For the return shipment, the customer is responsible for any export or re-import customs requirements in their country. We recommend contacting your local customs office for guidance before shipping an international return.

---

**Q: How long does an international return take to process?**
A: International returns take significantly longer than domestic returns:
- Return shipping transit time: 7–21 business days depending on origin country
- Customs clearance on the return: 2–10 business days (varies widely)
- Warehouse inspection: 1–3 business days after receipt
- Refund processing: 3–7 business days after inspection
Total expected time from dispatch to refund: 3–8 weeks. We will keep you informed by email at each stage.

---

**Q: Do you offer local return options for international customers?**
A: In select regions, we partner with local return hubs that allow international customers to drop off returns domestically rather than shipping directly to our main warehouse. Eligible countries and drop-off locations are listed in our Returns Portal when you enter your shipping country. Using a local return hub significantly reduces international return shipping costs and timelines.

## SECTION 12 — RETURN FRAUD AND POLICY ABUSE

---

**Q: What is considered return fraud?**
A: Return fraud includes but is not limited to:
- Returning a used, damaged, or inferior product while claiming it is in original condition
- Wardrobing — purchasing items for single-use (an event, photoshoot, etc.) with the intent to return
- Returning a different item than what was purchased (item switching)
- Filing a false defective or damaged claim to obtain a free return label
- Submitting multiple refund and return requests for the same item
- Coordinating fraudulent returns with a third party
