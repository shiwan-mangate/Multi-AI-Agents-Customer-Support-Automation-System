## SECTION 1 — ACCOUNT CREATION & REGISTRATION
---

**Q: How do I create a new account?**  
A: To create a new account, visit the official website and click on the "Sign Up" or "Register" button. Fill in the required details such as your full name, email address, and a secure password. After submitting the form, a verification email will be sent to your registered email address. Click the verification link within 24 hours to activate your account. Once verified, you can log in and access all features.

**Q: Can I create multiple accounts with the same email address?**  
A: No. Each email address can be associated with only one account. If you already have an account registered to your email, you will need to log in using that account or use a different email address to create a new one. If you have forgotten your password, use the "Forgot Password" option on the login page.

**Q: What are the eligibility requirements to create an account?**  
A: To create an account, you must:
- Be at least 18 years of age (or the legal age in your country).
- Provide a valid, active email address.
- Agree to the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.
- Not have a previously banned or suspended account.

Accounts created on behalf of an organization must be registered by an authorized representative.

**Q: How do I verify my email address after sign-up?**  
A: After registration, check your inbox for a verification email from our support address. Click the "Verify Email" button or link in that email. If you don't see the email within a few minutes, check your spam or junk folder. If it still hasn't arrived, go to your account settings and click "Resend Verification Email."

**Q: Can I sign up using a social account like Google or Facebook?**  
A: Yes. We support social sign-in via Google and, in some regions, Facebook. On the sign-up page, click "Continue with Google" or "Continue with Facebook." You will be redirected to the respective provider for authentication. No separate password is required — your social account credentials are used to authenticate you securely.

## SECTION 2 — LOGIN & AUTHENTICATION
---

**Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?**  
A: On the login page, click "Forgot Password?" and enter your registered email address. You will receive a password reset link within a few minutes. Click the link and enter a new password. The reset link is valid for 30 minutes. If the link expires, request a new one using the same process.

**Q: My account is locked. What should I do?**  
A: Accounts are temporarily locked after multiple consecutive failed login attempts, usually five or more within a short period, as a security measure. The lock is lifted automatically after 15–30 minutes. If you need immediate access, contact our support team and they can unlock your account after verifying your identity.

**Q: How do I enable two-factor authentication (2FA)?**  
A: Go to Account Settings → Security → Two-Factor Authentication and toggle it on. You can choose between an authenticator app (such as Google Authenticator or Authy) or SMS-based OTP. Follow the on-screen steps to link your preferred method. Once enabled, you will be prompted for a second verification code every time you log in from a new device.

**Q: Can I stay logged in on my device?**  
A: Yes. On the login page, check the "Remember Me" or "Keep me logged in" option. This keeps your session active for up to 30 days on that device. For security, we recommend not using this option on shared or public devices. You can view and revoke active sessions from Account Settings → Security → Active Sessions.

**Q: How do I log out from all devices at once?**  
A: Go to Account Settings → Security → Active Sessions. You will see a list of all devices currently logged into your account. Click "Log Out of All Devices" to terminate all sessions simultaneously. This is useful if you suspect unauthorized access to your account.

## SECTION 3 — ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT
---

**Q: How do I update my email address?**  
A: Go to Account Settings → Profile → Email Address and click "Change Email." Enter your new email address and confirm it. A verification link will be sent to the new address. Click the link to confirm the change. Your old email address will receive a notification about this update. The change takes effect only after the new email is verified.

**Q: How do I change my password?**  
A: Go to Account Settings → Security → Change Password. Enter your current password, followed by a new password and a confirmation of the new password. Your new password must be at least 8 characters and include a mix of uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols. Click "Save Changes" to apply the update.

**Q: How do I update my name or profile information?**  
A: Navigate to Account Settings → Profile. You can update your display name, phone number, profile picture, and other personal details. Click "Save" after making changes. Note that the account username or handle (if applicable) may have separate rules regarding how often it can be changed.

**Q: How do I close or deactivate my account?**  
A: Go to Account Settings → Account → Close Account. You will be asked to confirm the reason for closure and to re-enter your password. Upon confirmation, your account will be deactivated immediately. In most cases, account data is retained for 30 days before permanent deletion, allowing you to reactivate within that window if you change your mind. After 30 days, all data is permanently erased.

**Q: Can I reactivate a closed account?**  
A: Yes, if you closed your account within the last 30 days, it can be reactivated. Log in with your previous credentials, and you will be prompted to confirm reactivation. If more than 30 days have passed, the account and all associated data have been permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.

**Q: How do I transfer my account to someone else?**  
A: Account transfers are not supported for individual accounts, as each account is tied to a specific identity and email. For business or organization accounts, an admin can change the primary owner from the Admin Settings panel. If you need further assistance, contact our support team.

## SECTION 4 — SUBSCRIPTION PLANS
---

**Q: What subscription plans are available?**  
A: We offer the following plans:

| Plan | Best For | Billing Cycle |
|---|---|---|
| **Free** | Individuals, basic use | No charge |
| **Starter** | Freelancers and small teams | Monthly or Annual |
| **Pro** | Growing businesses | Monthly or Annual |
| **Enterprise** | Large organizations | Annual (custom pricing) |

Each plan differs in usage limits, features, integrations, and support levels. Visit the Pricing page for a detailed comparison.

**Q: How do I upgrade my subscription plan?**  
A: Go to Account Settings → Billing → Manage Subscription and click "Upgrade Plan." Select the desired plan and confirm the change. If upgrading mid-billing cycle, you will be charged a prorated amount for the remaining days in the current period. The new plan features are available immediately upon upgrade.

**Q: How do I downgrade my subscription plan?**  
A: Go to Account Settings → Billing → Manage Subscription and choose a lower-tier plan. The downgrade will take effect at the end of your current billing cycle — you will retain access to your current plan features until then. No partial refunds are issued for unused days.

**Q: What happens to my data if I downgrade to the Free plan?**  
A: When downgrading to the Free plan, features and data that exceed the Free plan limits will become inaccessible. For example, if your Free plan allows 3 projects but you currently have 10, the extra 7 projects will be archived and hidden (not deleted). You can access them again by upgrading. We recommend reviewing the Free plan limits before downgrading.

**Q: Is there a free trial for paid plans?**  
A: Yes. New users can start a 14-day free trial on the Pro plan without providing a credit card. At the end of the trial, you will be asked to choose a paid plan or continue on the Free plan. If you add a payment method during the trial, your card will not be charged until the trial period ends.

**Q: Can I pause my subscription instead of cancelling it?**  
A: Subscription pausing is available on Pro and Enterprise plans. You can pause billing for up to 3 months per calendar year. During the pause, your account and data remain intact, but access to paid features is suspended. To pause, go to Account Settings → Billing → Manage Subscription → Pause Subscription.

## SECTION 5 — PAYMENTS & INVOICES
---

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**  
A: We accept the following payment methods:
- Credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, RuPay)
- UPI (for India-based accounts)
- Net banking (for India-based accounts)
- PayPal
- Bank transfer / wire transfer (Enterprise plans only)

Cryptocurrency payments are not currently supported.

**Q: When will I be charged?**  
A: For monthly plans, billing occurs on the same date each month starting from the date you subscribed. For annual plans, you are billed once per year on your subscription anniversary date. If your payment date falls on a day that doesn't exist in a particular month (e.g., the 31st), billing occurs on the last day of that month.

**Q: How do I update my payment method?**  
A: Go to Account Settings → Billing → Payment Methods. Click "Add New Payment Method," enter the new card or payment details, and set it as the default. To remove an old payment method, select it and click "Remove." Ensure you always have at least one valid payment method on file to avoid service interruption.

**Q: What happens if my payment fails?**  
A: If a payment fails, we will retry the charge up to 3 times over the next 7 days. You will receive email notifications at each failed attempt. If the payment is not resolved within 7 days, your account will be downgraded to the Free plan and paid features will be suspended. Updating your payment method at any point triggers an immediate retry.

**Q: How do I download or view my invoices?**  
A: Go to Account Settings → Billing → Invoice History. All past invoices are listed there with date, amount, and status. Click on any invoice to view or download it as a PDF. Invoices can also be forwarded to a specific billing email by adding a billing address under Billing Settings.

**Q: Can I get an invoice with my company's GST or tax information?**  
A: Yes. Go to Account Settings → Billing → Tax Information and enter your GSTIN (for India), VAT number (for EU/UK), or equivalent tax ID. Once saved, all future invoices will include your tax details. To update tax details on a past invoice, contact our billing support team.

**Q: Are prices inclusive of taxes?**  
A: Prices displayed on the Pricing page are exclusive of applicable taxes unless otherwise stated. For Indian customers, GST (18%) is added at checkout. For EU/UK customers, VAT is applied based on your country's rate. The final amount including taxes is shown on the checkout screen before payment confirmation.

**Q: Do you support annual billing and is there a discount?**  
A: Yes. Choosing an annual plan saves you up to 20% compared to monthly billing. The full annual amount is charged upfront. The discount is applied automatically at checkout when you select the "Annual" billing option. Annual subscribers also get priority customer support access.

## SECTION 6 — REFUNDS & CANCELLATIONS
---

**Q: What is the refund policy?**  
A: We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for new paid subscriptions. If you are unsatisfied within the first 7 days of your initial paid period, contact our billing support with your account email and reason, and a full refund will be processed within 5–7 business days. Refunds are not available after 7 days or for renewal charges.

**Q: How do I cancel my subscription?**  
A: Go to Account Settings → Billing → Manage Subscription → Cancel Subscription. Follow the prompts to confirm. Your subscription remains active until the end of the current billing period. After cancellation, you will be moved to the Free plan at the end of the cycle. You will not be charged again unless you re-subscribe.

**Q: Will I lose my data when I cancel my subscription?**  
A: No, cancelling your subscription does not immediately delete your data. You are moved to the Free plan at the end of your billing cycle. Data exceeding Free plan limits will be archived but not deleted for 90 days, giving you time to export or reconfigure. After 90 days, excess data may be permanently removed.

**Q: I was charged after cancelling. What should I do?**  
A: This can happen if the cancellation was processed after the billing date. Log into your account and check the cancellation date under Billing History. If the cancellation was confirmed before the charge date, contact our billing support team with the relevant invoice number and they will investigate and issue a refund if warranted.

**Q: Can I get a prorated refund if I cancel mid-cycle?**  
A: We do not offer prorated refunds for mid-cycle cancellations on monthly plans. However, on annual plans, if you cancel within the first 30 days, you may be eligible for a prorated refund for unused months (minus the first month's fee). Contact billing support to review your eligibility.

## SECTION 7 — TEAM & ORGANIZATION ACCOUNTS
---

**Q: How do I add team members to my account?**  
A: Go to Account Settings → Team → Invite Members. Enter the email addresses of people you want to invite and assign them a role (Admin, Editor, Viewer, etc.). Invitees will receive an email with a link to join your workspace. Each invited member counts against your plan's seat limit.

**Q: How is billing handled for team plans?**  
A: Team plan billing is based on the number of active seats in your workspace. You are billed per seat per month (or per year on annual plans). When you add a new member mid-cycle, a prorated charge is applied immediately for that billing period. When you remove a member, the seat is released at the end of the current cycle.

**Q: What happens if I exceed my plan's seat limit?**  
A: If you try to invite more members than your current plan allows, you will be prompted to upgrade to a higher plan or purchase additional seats. Existing members will not be affected. You can view your current seat usage under Account Settings → Billing → Seat Usage.

**Q: How do I remove a team member from my workspace?**  
A: Go to Account Settings → Team → Members. Find the member you wish to remove and click "Remove from Workspace." Their access will be revoked immediately. The seat will be freed at the end of the current billing cycle. Any content they created remains in the workspace and is reassigned to the workspace admin.

**Q: Can I set different roles and permissions for team members?**  
A: Yes. Role-based access control is available on Starter and above plans. Common roles include:
- **Admin** — Full access including billing and member management.
- **Editor** — Can create and edit content but cannot manage billing.
- **Viewer** — Read-only access.
- **Custom Roles** (Enterprise only) — Define granular permissions as needed.

Roles can be updated anytime from Account Settings → Team → Members.

## SECTION 8 — SECURITY & PRIVACY
---

**Q: How is my billing information secured?**  
A: All payment information is encrypted using TLS in transit and stored in compliance with PCI-DSS standards. We do not store full credit card numbers on our servers. Payment processing is handled through our certified third-party payment processor. Your CVV is never stored at any point.

**Q: Has my account been compromised? How do I check?**  
A: Go to Account Settings → Security → Login Activity to view recent login history. If you see unfamiliar IP addresses, locations, or devices, your account may have been accessed without authorization. Immediately change your password, enable 2FA, and log out of all devices. Then contact our security support team.

**Q: How do I report a suspicious charge on my account?**  
A: If you notice an unrecognized charge, go to Account Settings → Billing → Invoice History and review all transactions. If a charge is not recognized, contact our billing support with the invoice number, amount, and date. We will investigate within 2 business days. For suspected fraudulent activity, also contact your bank or card issuer.

**Q: What data does the platform collect from my account?**  
A: We collect the personal information you provide during registration (name, email, phone number), payment details (handled by our PCI-compliant payment processor), usage data (features accessed, session duration), and device/browser metadata for security and analytics. Full details are available in our Privacy Policy. You can request a data export or deletion at any time from Account Settings → Privacy.

**Q: Can I request deletion of all my personal data?**  
A: Yes. Under GDPR, CCPA, and applicable data protection regulations, you have the right to request deletion of your personal data. Go to Account Settings → Privacy → Request Data Deletion or submit a formal deletion request to our privacy team. Processing typically takes up to 30 days. Note that some data may be retained for legal and financial compliance for up to 7 years (e.g., transaction records).

## SECTION 9 — PROMOTIONS, COUPONS & CREDITS
---

**Q: How do I apply a promo code or coupon?**  
A: On the checkout or plan upgrade page, look for the "Promo Code" or "Coupon Code" field. Enter your code and click "Apply." The discount will be reflected in the order summary before you confirm payment. Promo codes are case-insensitive and can only be applied once per account unless otherwise specified.

**Q: Why isn't my promo code working?**  
A: Common reasons a promo code may not work:
- The code has expired.
- The code is not applicable to your selected plan.
- The code has already been used on your account.
- The code is valid only for new users and you have an existing account.
- There is a typo or extra space in the code.

If you believe the code should be valid, contact support with the code and a screenshot of the error.

**Q: How do account credits work?**  
A: Account credits are a form of balance applied to your account that are used before charging your payment method. Credits can be earned through referrals, promotional campaigns, or issued as compensation for service issues. Your credit balance is displayed in Account Settings → Billing → Credits & Balance. Credits are applied automatically on the next billing cycle and are non-refundable.

**Q: Do you have a referral program?**  
A: Yes. You can refer others using your unique referral link found under Account Settings → Referrals. When a referred user subscribes to a paid plan, you earn a credit equivalent to one month of their plan value, applied to your account. Referral credits are credited within 7 days of the referred user's first successful payment.

## SECTION 10 — ENTERPRISE & CUSTOM PLANS
---

**Q: How do I get a custom Enterprise plan quote?**  
A: Visit the Pricing page and click "Contact Sales" under the Enterprise section. Fill out the inquiry form with your organization's name, size, use case, and contact details. A sales representative will reach out within 1–2 business days to discuss your requirements and provide a tailored quote.

**Q: Does the Enterprise plan support single sign-on (SSO)?**  
A: Yes. Enterprise plans support SSO via SAML 2.0, allowing integration with identity providers such as Okta, Microsoft Azure AD, and Google Workspace. SSO setup requires coordination with our technical onboarding team. To get started, contact your account manager or reach out via enterprise support.

**Q: Can I get a dedicated account manager?**  
A: Dedicated account managers are available for Enterprise plan customers. Your account manager serves as the primary point of contact for onboarding, technical issues, billing queries, and strategic guidance. If you are on a Pro plan, you have access to priority support but not a dedicated manager. Contact sales to inquire about Enterprise eligibility.

**Q: Is a Service Level Agreement (SLA) available?**  
A: Yes. Formal SLAs are available with Enterprise plans. These typically include guaranteed uptime percentages (e.g., 99.9% or higher), defined response times for support tickets (e.g., 1-hour response for critical issues), and escalation procedures. The SLA terms are negotiated as part of the Enterprise contract. Contact our sales team for details.
