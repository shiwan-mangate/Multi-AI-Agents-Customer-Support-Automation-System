## SECTION 1 — SUBSCRIPTION OVERVIEW

---

**Q: What is a subscription plan?**
A: A subscription plan is a recurring billing arrangement where you pay a set fee on a regular schedule (monthly or annually) in exchange for ongoing access to our products, services, or member benefits. Unlike a one-time purchase, a subscription automatically renews at the end of each billing cycle until you choose to cancel. Subscriptions may include benefits such as unlimited access to content, discounted pricing, free shipping, priority support, exclusive features, or a combination of these depending on the plan.

---

**Q: What subscription plans do you offer?**
A: We currently offer the following subscription tiers:

- **Basic Plan:** Entry-level access with core features. Billed monthly at $9.99/month or annually at $99.99/year (save 17%).
- **Standard Plan:** Full-feature access including priority support and additional usage limits. Billed monthly at $19.99/month or annually at $199.99/year (save 17%).
- **Premium Plan:** All Standard features plus exclusive perks, dedicated account manager, and advanced integrations. Billed monthly at $49.99/month or annually at $499.99/year (save 17%).
- **Enterprise Plan:** Custom pricing for teams of 10 or more, with tailored features, SLA guarantees, and centralized billing. Contact our sales team for a quote.

Plan features, limits, and pricing are subject to change. The most current plan details are always available on our Pricing page.

---

**Q: What is the difference between a monthly and an annual subscription?**
A: A monthly subscription is billed every 30 days and can be cancelled at any time with effect from the end of the current billing period. An annual subscription is billed once per year (365 days) at a discounted rate (typically 17% less than paying monthly for 12 months). Annual subscriptions can be cancelled but are non-refundable after the first 30 days, and access continues through the end of the paid year.

---

**Q: Can I try the service before subscribing?**
A: Yes. We offer a 14-day free trial for new subscribers on our Basic and Standard plans. No payment is required to start the trial. At the end of the 14-day period, if you choose to continue, your subscription will begin and your payment method will be charged automatically. You may cancel the trial at any time before the trial period ends without being charged. Free trials are available to new accounts only — one trial per household or billing address.

---

**Q: Is a credit card required to start a free trial?**
A: A valid payment method is required to start the free trial so that your subscription can begin automatically if you choose to continue. Your card will not be charged during the trial period. If you cancel before the trial ends, no charge will be made. We accept credit cards, debit cards, PayPal, and select digital wallets. Prepaid cards may be accepted but are not recommended as they may not support recurring billing.

---

**Q: Are there any setup fees or hidden charges?**
A: No. There are no setup fees, activation fees, or hidden charges associated with any of our subscription plans. The price shown on our Pricing page is the exact amount you will be billed per cycle. Applicable taxes are calculated and displayed at checkout before you confirm your subscription. You will never be billed an amount different from what was confirmed at signup without prior notice.

---

**Q: Do you offer a family or group subscription plan?**
A: Yes. Our Family Plan allows up to 5 members under a single subscription at $29.99/month or $299.99/year. Each member gets their own individual account profile with personal settings and usage history. The primary account holder manages billing and can add or remove family members from the account dashboard. Family Plan members must reside in the same country as the primary account holder.

---

**Q: Do you offer student or nonprofit discounts?**
A: Yes. We offer the following discounted plans:
- **Student Discount:** 50% off the Standard Plan for verified students. Requires annual verification via your institutional email address or a supported student verification service.
- **Nonprofit Discount:** 30% off all plans for registered nonprofit organizations. Requires submission of proof of nonprofit status (e.g., IRS determination letter for US organizations, or equivalent for other countries). Contact our nonprofit support team at nonprofit@company.com to apply.

## SECTION 2 — BILLING AND PAYMENT

---

**Q: When am I charged for my subscription?**
A: For monthly subscriptions, you are charged on the same date each month as your original signup date (e.g., if you subscribed on the 15th, you are billed on the 15th of every subsequent month). For annual subscriptions, you are charged once per year on the anniversary of your original signup date. You will receive a billing reminder email 7 days before each renewal charge.

---

**Q: What payment methods are accepted for subscriptions?**
A: We accept the following payment methods for subscription billing:
- Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover (credit and debit)
- PayPal (linked to a bank account or card)
- Apple Pay and Google Pay (on supported devices and browsers)
- Bank transfer / ACH (available for annual plans only, with a minimum of $100/year)
- Cryptocurrency (USDC and BTC accepted for annual plans via our crypto billing portal)

Prepaid gift cards and one-time virtual cards are not suitable for subscription billing as they cannot support recurring charges.

---

**Q: Can I change my payment method?**
A: Yes. You can update your payment method at any time from your account settings under "Billing" → "Payment Method." Changes take effect immediately and will apply to the next billing cycle. If a payment fails due to an outdated payment method, you will have a 7-day grace period to update your details before your subscription is paused. We recommend updating your payment method before it expires to avoid service interruption.

---

**Q: What happens if my payment fails?**
A: If a subscription payment fails (due to insufficient funds, expired card, or bank decline), we will:
1. Notify you by email immediately with a link to update your payment method.
2. Retry the charge automatically after 3 days.
3. Retry a second time after 7 days if the first retry also fails.
4. If all three attempts fail, your subscription will be paused and access restricted.
5. You have 14 days from the first failed payment to resolve the issue before the subscription is cancelled. All data and settings are retained during the grace period.

---

**Q: Will I be notified before each renewal charge?**
A: Yes. We send an automatic renewal reminder email 7 days before each billing date. The email includes the amount to be charged, the date of the charge, and a direct link to manage or cancel your subscription. For annual renewals, we send an additional reminder 30 days in advance. These reminders are sent to the primary email address on your account.

---

**Q: Can I get an invoice for my subscription payment?**
A: Yes. An invoice is automatically generated for every subscription payment and sent to the email address on your account. You can also download PDF invoices for any billing period from your account portal under "Billing" → "Invoice History." Business and enterprise customers can request invoices with company name, VAT number, and purchase order references by updating their billing profile.

---

**Q: Can I pay for my subscription using multiple payment methods?**
A: No. Each subscription can only be associated with one primary payment method at a time. If you wish to use a different payment method for a specific billing cycle, update your payment method before the billing date under "Billing" → "Payment Method." Split payment across multiple methods is not supported for subscription billing.

---

**Q: Do subscription prices include taxes?**
A: Subscription prices displayed on our pricing page are exclusive of applicable taxes. Taxes (sales tax, VAT, GST, etc.) are calculated based on your billing address and displayed separately at checkout before you confirm your subscription. Tax rates may change in response to local legislation, and any such changes will be reflected in your invoice. We comply with all applicable tax laws in the jurisdictions where we operate.

**Q: What currency am I billed in?**
A: You are billed in the local currency of your country where supported. Currently supported billing currencies include USD, EUR, GBP, CAD, AUD, INR, SGD, JPY, and BRL. If your country's currency is not supported, billing defaults to USD. Currency selection is set at the time of account creation and cannot be changed without cancelling and restarting the subscription.

## SECTION 3 — PLAN CHANGES AND UPGRADES

---

**Q: Can I upgrade my subscription plan?**
A: Yes. You can upgrade your plan at any time from your account settings under "Subscription" → "Change Plan." Upgrades take effect immediately. You will be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of the current billing cycle based on the price difference between your current and new plan. The full new plan price applies from the next billing cycle onwards.

---

**Q: How is the prorated charge calculated when I upgrade?**
A: The prorated charge is calculated as follows:
- We determine the number of days remaining in your current billing cycle.
- We calculate the daily rate of both the old and new plan.
- You are charged the difference in daily rate multiplied by the remaining days.

Example: You are on the Basic plan ($9.99/month) and upgrade to Standard ($19.99/month) with 15 days remaining in your cycle. Daily rate difference = ($19.99 − $9.99) / 30 = $0.333/day. Prorated charge = $0.333 × 15 = $5.00. Your next full billing cycle will be charged at the Standard plan rate.

---

**Q: Can I downgrade my subscription plan?**
A: Yes. You can downgrade your plan at any time, but downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle — not immediately. This means you retain access to your current plan's features until the end of the current billing period. No prorated refund is issued for the unused portion of a higher-tier plan when downgrading. Feature access will adjust to the new plan's limits at the start of the next cycle.

---

**Q: What happens to my data and settings when I downgrade?**
A: When you downgrade, features and limits associated with the higher-tier plan will no longer be accessible. Specifically:
- Usage that exceeds the lower plan's limits will be locked (not deleted) until you upgrade again or reduce usage to within the new plan's limits.
- Data stored under premium features (e.g., advanced integrations, extra storage, API access) will be retained for 60 days in a read-only state, giving you time to export or reduce before it is permanently removed.
- You will receive notifications about any data at risk of removal before downgrading is finalized.

---

**Q: Can I switch from a monthly to an annual plan (or vice versa)?**
A: Yes. You can switch billing frequency at any time:
- **Monthly to Annual:** The switch takes effect immediately. You are charged the annual rate at the time of switch, and any unused days from your current monthly cycle are credited toward the annual plan as a prorated deduction.
- **Annual to Monthly:** The switch takes effect at the end of your current annual billing period. You will not be refunded for the remaining months on the annual plan. At renewal, you will be billed at the monthly rate.

---

**Q: Can I pause my subscription instead of downgrading or cancelling?**
A: Yes, subscription pausing is available once per 12-month period for up to 3 months. During a pause:
- Billing is suspended for the pause duration.
- Access to the service is restricted (read-only mode for most plans).
- Your data, settings, and account history are fully preserved.
- After the pause period ends, your subscription resumes automatically and billing restarts at your previous plan rate.
To pause your subscription, go to "Subscription" → "Pause Plan" in your account settings.

---

**Q: Can I add extra users or seats to my subscription?**
A: Yes. For Standard, Premium, and Enterprise plans, additional user seats can be added at any time. Each additional seat is billed at a prorated rate for the current cycle and at the full per-seat rate for subsequent cycles. Seat pricing is:
- Standard: $8.00/month per additional seat
- Premium: $18.00/month per additional seat
- Enterprise: Custom pricing — contact your account manager
On the Basic plan, additional seats are not available; you would need to upgrade to Standard or higher to add team members.

## SECTION 5 — CANCELLATION

---

**Q: How do I cancel my subscription?**
A: You can cancel your subscription at any time through the following methods:
1. Log in to your account → go to "Settings" → "Subscription" → click "Cancel Subscription" and follow the prompts.
2. Contact our support team via live chat, email, or phone and request cancellation.
3. Reply "CANCEL" to any billing notification email and a support agent will process the cancellation within 1 business day.
Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing cycle. You will retain full access to the service until then.

---

**Q: When does my access end after I cancel?**
A: After cancelling, your access continues until the last day of your current paid billing cycle. For example, if you are billed on the 1st of the month and cancel on the 10th, your access continues through the end of the month (the 31st). You will not be billed again after cancellation. Once access ends, your account data is retained in a limited state for 60 days before permanent deletion.

---

**Q: Will I get a refund when I cancel?**
A: Cancellation does not automatically trigger a refund:
- **Monthly plans:** No refund is issued for the remainder of the current billing period. Access continues until the cycle ends.
- **Annual plans:** Refunds for annual plans are available within the first 30 days of purchase (pro-rated for the unused portion). After 30 days, no refund is issued but access continues through the end of the paid year.
- **Free trial cancellations:** No charge is made, and no refund is applicable.
See the Refund Policy section for exceptions (billing errors, unauthorized charges, etc.).

---

**Q: Is there a cancellation fee?**
A: No. There is no cancellation fee for any of our subscription plans. You may cancel at any time without penalty. We do not lock you into long-term contracts (except for Enterprise plans with custom agreements, where contract terms govern). We believe in earning your continued business through value, not through punitive cancellation fees.

---

**Q: Can I cancel mid-cycle and get a partial refund for the unused days?**
A: For monthly plans, partial refunds for unused days are not standard policy. However, if you have extenuating circumstances (e.g., serious illness, financial hardship, or a significant service outage that prevented you from using the service), contact our support team and we will evaluate your request on a case-by-case basis. Annual plan cancellations within the first 30 days are prorated — see the Refund Policy.

---

**Q: What happens to my data after I cancel?**
A: After cancellation, your account and all associated data (profile, history, saved preferences, files, and settings) are retained in a dormant state for 60 days. During this period, you can reactivate your subscription and regain full access to your data. After 60 days, your data is permanently and irreversibly deleted in accordance with our Privacy Policy. To export your data before deletion, use the "Export My Data" option in account settings.

---

**Q: Can I reactivate a cancelled subscription?**
A: Yes. You can reactivate your subscription at any time within 60 days of cancellation without losing your data or settings. To reactivate, log in to your account and click "Reactivate Subscription" under "Settings" → "Subscription." Your billing will resume at the standard plan rate from the reactivation date. If you reactivate after 60 days, a new account will need to be created as your previous data will have been deleted.

---

**Q: I cancelled but I'm still being charged. What do I do?**
A: If you are being charged after confirming your cancellation, this is likely a billing error. Contact our support team immediately with:
- Your account email
- The date you cancelled (and your cancellation confirmation email if available)
- Details of the charge(s) in question
We will investigate, halt any further charges, and refund any erroneous charges within 3–5 business days. We recommend always saving your cancellation confirmation email as proof.

---

**Q: Can I cancel my subscription over the phone?**
A: Yes. You can cancel by calling our customer support line at 1-800-XXX-XXXX (Mon–Fri, 9 AM–6 PM). A support agent will process the cancellation and send you a confirmation email. Please have your account email address and/or subscription ID ready. We do not make cancellation deliberately difficult — if you request cancellation, it will be processed promptly without pressure to stay.

## SECTION 6 — AUTO-RENEWAL

---

**Q: What is auto-renewal?**
A: Auto-renewal is the automatic continuation of your subscription at the end of each billing cycle without requiring action from you. When auto-renewal is active, your payment method is charged for the next cycle before the current cycle ends, ensuring uninterrupted access to the service. Auto-renewal is enabled by default for all subscriptions and is disclosed clearly at signup.

---

**Q: How do I turn off auto-renewal?**
A: You can disable auto-renewal at any time from "Settings" → "Subscription" → toggle "Auto-Renewal" to off. Disabling auto-renewal does not cancel your subscription immediately — it schedules your subscription to end at the conclusion of the current billing cycle. You will receive a confirmation email when auto-renewal is turned off, and a reminder before your access ends.

---

**Q: Will I be reminded before my subscription auto-renews?**
A: Yes. We send renewal reminder emails:
- 30 days before annual renewal
- 7 days before monthly renewal
- 3 days before renewal for accounts with previously failed payments

Each reminder includes the renewal amount, billing date, and a direct link to manage your subscription. If you do not wish to continue, you can cancel directly from the reminder email.

---

**Q: I didn't realize my annual subscription was about to renew. Can I get a refund?**
A: We make every effort to remind you of upcoming annual renewals (30-day and 7-day notices). However, if you missed the reminders and were charged for an annual renewal you did not intend, contact support within 7 days of the charge. If the service has not been used in the new annual period, we will evaluate a prorated or full refund as a one-time courtesy. This is not guaranteed and is assessed individually.

---

**Q: What if my payment method expires before auto-renewal?**
A: If your payment method is set to expire before your next renewal date, we will send you an alert email 14 days in advance prompting you to update your payment details. If the payment method expires and the renewal charge fails, your subscription enters a 7-day grace period during which you can update your payment method to avoid interruption. After 7 days without a successful payment, access is paused and the subscription is placed in delinquent status.

**Q: Can I manage my subscription from a mobile device?**

---

**Q: Is my payment information stored securely?**

What we store is a secure token provided by the payment processor, which is used to facilitate recurring billing without exposing your card data.
